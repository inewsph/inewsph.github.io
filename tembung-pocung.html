1. Watake Tembang Maskumambang

Watake = Sedih lan nelangsa

Cirine = 12i – 6a – 8i – 8o

2. Watake Tembang Mijil

Watake = Terbuka

Cirine = 10i – 6o – 10e – 10i – 6i – 6o

3. Watake Tembang Kinanthi

Watake = Seneng, nasehat, kasih sayang, teladan ingkang becik.

Cirine = 8u – 8i – 8a – 8i – 8a – 8i

4. Watake Tembang Sinom

Watake = Sabar lan Ramah Tamah

Cirine = 8a – 8i – 8a – 8i – 7i – 8u – 7a – 8i – 12a

5. Watake Tembang Asmarandhana

Watake = Asmara, Asmara Ingkang Nelangsa

Cirine = 8i – 8a – 8e – 7a – 8a – 8u – 8a

6. Watake Tembang Gambuh

Watake = Kekancan

Cirine = 7u – 10u – 12i – 8u – 8o

7. Watake Tembang Dhandhanggula

Watake = Watak-watake umum, luwes, lan melebet ing ati.

Cirine = 10i – 10a – 8e – 7u – 9i – 7a – 6u – 8a – 12i – 7a

8. Watake Tembang Durma

Watake = Tegas, Keras, Menggebu-gebu

Cirine = 12a – 7i – 6a – 7a – 8i – 5a – 7i

9. Watake Tembang Pangkur

Watake = Gagah, Kuat, Prakosa

Cirine = 8a – 11i – 8u – 7a – 8i – 5a – 7i

10. Watake Tembang Megatruh

Watake = Kilangan, kesedihan, putus asa.

Cirine = 12u – 8i – 8u – 8i – 8o

11. Watake Tembang Pucung

Watake = Geguyon, nasehat, bebas

Cirine = 12u – 6a – 8i – 12a
