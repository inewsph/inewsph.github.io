6. buat kalimat dialog masing masing 7 kalimat will. b.inggris​


Dialog 1:

Person A: Hi Will, how are you today?

Person B: I'm doing well, thanks for asking. How about you?

Person A: I'm good, thanks. So, what did you do over the weekend?

Person B: I went on a hike with some friends. It was really beautiful.

Person A: That sounds like fun. Did you take any pictures?

Person B: Yes, I did. I'll show them to you later.

Person A: Great, I'd love to see them.

Dialog 2:

Person A: Will, have you seen the new movie that just came out?

Person B: No, I haven't had a chance to go to the cinema lately. Is it any good?

Person A: Yes, it's really great. You should definitely see it.

Person B: Okay, I'll try to make time for it this weekend.

Person A: That sounds like a good plan. Let me know what you think of it.

Person B: Will do. Thanks for the recommendation.

Person A: No problem, anytime.

Dialog 3:

Person A: Will, do you want to go grab some lunch together?

Person B: Sure, that sounds like a good idea. Where do you want to go?

Person A: How about that new sushi place downtown?

Person B: Sounds good to me. What time do you want to go?

Person A: How about in an hour?

Person B: That works for me. Let's meet there at noon.

Person A: Perfect. I'll see you then.

Dialog 4:

Person A: Will, I need some advice. I'm not sure what to do about my job.

Person B: What's going on? Are you unhappy there?

Person A: Yeah, I am. I feel like I'm not being challenged enough.

Person B: Have you talked to your boss about it?

Person A: No, I haven't. I'm not sure how to bring it up.

Person B: I think you should schedule a meeting with your boss and express your concerns.

Person A: Okay, that's a good idea. I'll do that. Thanks for your help.

Person B: Anytime. Let me know how it goes.

Dialog 5:

Person A: Will, have you ever traveled to Europe before?

Person B: Yes, I have. I went to Spain a few years ago.

Person A: That's cool. I've never been to Europe before. What was your favorite thing about Spain?

Person B: I really enjoyed the food and the architecture. The Gaudi buildings were amazing.

Person A: That sounds awesome. I've always wanted to try Spanish food. What do you recommend?

Person B: Definitely try the paella and the churros. They're delicious.

Person A: Thanks for the tips. I'll make sure to try them when I go to Spain.

Dialog 6:

Person A: Will, what did you study in college?

Person B: I studied computer science. How about you?

Person A: I studied psychology. What made you choose computer science?

Person B: I've always been interested in technology and programming. It seemed like a natural fit for me.

Person A: That's really cool. Do you enjoy your job in the tech industry?

Person B: Yes, I do. It's constantly evolving and there's always something new to learn.

Person A: That's great. I'm glad you found something you enjoy.

Dialog 7:

Person A: Will, what are your plans for the weekend?

Person B: I'm not sure yet. I was thinking of maybe going for a bike ride.

Person A: That sounds like a good idea. Do you want some
