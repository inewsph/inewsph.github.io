21. contoh dialog yang berisi asking,giving,offering,and rejecting help!!!


offering
Lita : Hey guys! What are you going to do ?
Seno : I will going to water my flower ! Nevertheless, I must take my mother to see the doctor . She got headache .
Lita : Let me help you to water your flower . I will glad if I can help you .
Seno : That ’s very kind of you, but I think I can manage it .
Lita : Hey , just take it easy . I will help you .
Seno : No , thanks.

Asking
Lita : Seno , would you like to take a cap for me? It ’s so hot here .
Seno : I ’m sorry Lita . I am hurry to take my mother to see the doctor .
Lita : Where is the cap ? I will take it by myself .
Seno : The cap is on the cupboard in the kitchen . You can find it , right ?
LIta : I wonder I can to find it . Will you accompany me to take it ?
Seno : Not for now Lita . I must go now . Try to look for it by yourself .
Lita : Okay . I will . Take care on your way .

Giving
Rama : Dendi, why you don’t go to the zoo with your friends ?
Dendi : Hmm..I’m sorry I can’t told you the reason. I’m very sad.
Rama : Why Dendi ? Don’t worry. I will keep your secret.
Dendi : Really ? Well, I don’t go to the zoo with my friends because I don’t have money.
Rama : Really ? Why you don’t tell me ? May I help you ?
Dendi : Hmmm. Don’t bother yourself, thanks . I’m afraid if I can’t give the money back to you if I borrow your money.
Rama : Don’t worry Dendi. We are friends. You can give it back to me just when you can.
Dendi : Oh My God. Thank you Rama. You are my best friend.
Rama : Yeah. Never mind.

rejecting
you : Mother, would you help me? (asking for help)
your mother : Sure. I would be glad to help you (accepting for help)
you : I can't do my homework. Could you explain it for me, Mom? (asking for help)
your mother : I wish I could help you but I don't understand this subject, my dear. I'm sorry. (refusing for help)
you : It's okay, Mom. I'll find it again myself.
your mother : What can I do for you, dear? (offering help)
you : Thanks, Mom, but I can manage myself. (refusing help)
your mother : Okay. Be patient, my dear.

28. Ada yang bisa buat dialog OFFERING help/service about covid19? minimum 10parts


Jawaban:

A: Can COVID-19 spread through food?

B: Coronaviruses cannot multiply in food; they need an animal or human host to multiply.

A: Will I get more severe symptoms of COVID-19 if I drink alcohol?

B: Consuming alcohol will not destroy the virus, and its consumption is likely to increase the health risks if a person becomes infected with the virus.

A: Is the coronavirus disease more severe than the flu?

B: While many people globally have built up immunity to seasonal flu strains, COVID-19 is a new virus to which no one has immunity. That means more people are susceptible to infection, and some will suffer severe disease.

A: How dangerous is COVID-19?

B: Although for most people COVID-19 causes only mild illness, it can make some people very ill. More rarely, the disease can be fatal. Older people, and those with pre- existing medical conditions (such as high blood pressure, heart problems or diabetes) appear to be more vulnerable

A: What are some safety precautions against the coronavirus disease?

B: By following good respiratory hygiene, you protect the people around you from viruses such as cold, flu and COVID-19.

Stay home and self-isolate even with minor symptoms such as cough, headache, mild fever, until you recover. Have someone bring you supplies. If you need to leave your house, wear a mask to avoid infecting others
