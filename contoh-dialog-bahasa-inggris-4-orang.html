2. contoh dialog singkat dalam bahasa inggris 4 orang


Dialog singkat dalam bahasa Inggris 4 orang:

Joni: Hi, Jeni. What do you think about English test today?

Jeni: Frankly, I nailed it.

Rita: Wow, really?

Tina: It was hard, but easy if you prepared for the test.

Jeni: That's right, Tina. English is easy. It's just words, just like Bahasa Indonesia.

Joni: I didn't study last night. I think I'll get bad score.

Jeni: Hey, don't think about the score. Just think about how you can improve your skill in English.

Tina: Jeni is right. English is very useful for us. It's an international language. Even in many countries English is the second language spoken beside their mother tongue.

Rita: But how? The grammar is too difficult.

Jeni: The grammar is easy. Don't just memorize it. You must practice. I mean a lot.

Joni: Well, I think you both have to teach us English.

Rita: Yeah, good idea! Let's practice together!

Pembahasan

Dialog singkat di atas bertema ujian bahasa Inggris. Dalam dialog tersebut ada 4 empat orang yaitu: Joni, Jeni, Rita, dan Tina. Dalam dialog tersebut mereka membicarakan tentang ujian bahasa Inggris yang mereka ikuti. Jeni dan Tina bisa mengerjakannya dengan mudah. Joni dan Rita kemudian meminta bantuan mereka agar mau belajar bahasa Inggris bersama.

11. Contoh dialog bahasa inggris tentang gempa bumi di palu 4 orang


Contoh dialog bahasa Inggris tentang gempa bumi di Palu untuk 4 orang adalah sebagai berikut.


Lala: Hey guys, have you heard about the latest news?

Anna: Which one?

Putri: I bet the one that just happened yesterday in Palu?

Lala: Yeah, exactly! I was very surprising!

Hans: I have my family there but thank God they are all safe and sound.

Lala: That’s great.

Putri: Anybody knows about the detail of the earthquake?

Anna: Well, as far as I’m reading from the newspaper, it’s a 7.5 magnitude earthquake and followed by a tsunami with waves up to 6m high.

Hans: That’s true. And you guys also need to know why our country is prone to earthquake. It’s because Indonesia lies on the Ring of Fire, which is the line of frequent quakes and volcanic eruptions that circles virtually the entire Pacific rim.

Putri: I see! Thanks a lot for the info guys. Let’s just pray for the victims and all.

Lala: Agree.


Pembahasan

Untuk membuat suatu dialog yang berkaitan dengan berita-berita aktual, kalian dapat mengimprovisasi dengan memasukan beberapa informasi dari dalam berita ke bagian dialog.


Seperti pada dialog di atas, agar tidak terdengar general/umum, kita dapat menambahkan informasi dari unsur 5W + 1H, seperti seberapa besar gempa, berapa tinggi tsunami, mengapa Indonesia sering terjadi gempa, dll.


Semua informasi yang bisa membantu dialog kalian dapat diraih melalui news item.
