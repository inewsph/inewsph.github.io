4. Contoh paragraf yang menggunakan simple past tense dan present tense


Past Tense

( Menceritakan perbuatan / peristiwa yang terjadi beberapa saat yang lalu atau pada masa yang telah lampau )


Rumus

+ ) S + V2 + O

- ) S + did + not + V1 + O

? ) Did + S + V1 + O


Contoh paragraf yang menggunakan simple past tense


- Yesterday , we went to a zoo , i saw lot of animals there , the biggest animal that i saw is elephant , it had a long trunk , after visited the zoo , we went home , we slept well last night .

Peristiwa di atas sudah terjadi , lalu si pencerita menceritakan kembali kejadian tersebut , itu yang dinamakan Past Tense



Present Tense

Menceritakan kegiatan sehari - hari


Rumus

+ ) S + s/es/V1 + O

- ) S + do / does + not + V1 + O

? ) Do / does + S + V1 + O


V1 digunakan jika Subjectnya = I,You,We,They

s / es digunakan jika Subjectnya = He,She,It



Contoh paragraf menggunakan Present Tense

I go to school every day , After school , Me and My friends always play together at the GreenField , i also don't forget to do my homework , after i do my homework i go to bed at 8 P.M. every day .

JAWABAN ;

( in the school backyard )

Dee       : Zoe, our friends are arguing Gea's daily activity ! Do you know her?

Zoe       : Certainly, she is my neighbor. She is a good girl. She often helps her friends who need help. She helps her mother everyday. She usually comes early to school. She always sits on the first row seat . She is such a diligent girl. She loves reading any kind of books.

Dee       :  By the way , what is she doing now during the  school break?

Zoe       : She is reading some books in the school library right now. Do you want to speak to her? I will introduce you later.

Dee       ;  No, actually, i would like to lend a novel.

Zoe       ; okay, i can take you to her house after school. Isn't it the correct time ?

Dee      : Yes. Thank you for your kindness, zoe.

PENJELASAN :

SIMPLE PRESENT TENSE

=> MEMBAHAS TENTANG FAKTA KEBENARAN ( GENERAL TRUTH ) , FENOMENA

=> KEBIASAAN YANG DILAKUKAN BERULAN-ULANG ( HABITUAL ACITIVITY/ DAILY ACTIVITY )

=> MENGGUNAKAN KETERANGAN FREKUENSI =

ALWAYS, USUALLY, EVERYDAY, SELDOM, RARELY, OFTEN, TWICE A WEEK, ONCE A DAY

=> POLA/RUMUS

SUBJECT + VERB ( S/ES ) + OBJECT/COMPLEMENT  ( VERBAL SENTENCE )

SUBJECT +TOBE(IS/AM/ARE) + NOUN/ADJECTIVE/ADVERB  ( NOMINAL )

PRESENT CONTINUOUS TENSE

=> MEMBAHAS KEGIATAN YANG DILAKUKAN KETIKA SEDANG DIBICARAKAN SESAAT.

=>MENGGUNAKAN KETERANGAN WAKTU =

RIGHT NOW, NOW, STILL, AT THE MOMENT

=>POLA / RUMUS =

SUBJECT+TOBE(IS/AM/ARE) + VERB-ING + OBJECT/COMPLEMENT

a. Simple present

I am Sarah. My full name is Sarah Lau Smith. I am a second grade student of Junior High School. I am 14 years old. My birth day is on July 5.

In my family, I am the second child. I have an older brother named Nathan and one younger sister named Grace. My mother is from China and my father is from Australia. We live in Indonesia.

My family and I love pets. We have some pets at home, namely five fish, one dog and two cats. Nathan love cats but I love dogs. Our cats and dog are like friends. They never fight.

In the future, I want to be a veterinarian. I want to help animals to survive so they can live longer. I like watching their unique behaviors.  They are all beautiful creatures. I love animals.

b/ Simple past tense

During my last holiday, I made a lot of handcrafts. At that time, I was crazy about art and creativity. Many ideas had come to my mind three months before. All of the ideas were written on my note book so I could read them again. Also, I searched other ideas from internet. Yap, I googled it. I watched tutorial videos and read many art blogs.

My siblings joined me on the third day. Then, my siblings and I made paper crafts from used newspapers and magazines. Papers were rolled, shaped and glued together to create decorative designs. Many beautiful crafts were created.

The last activity was sewing. My mother is a tailor so I learn how to sew from her. My mother helped me too. Sewing used clothes and towels was very fun. We produced many useful things. Finally, I really enjoyed my last holiday.

c. simple future tense
