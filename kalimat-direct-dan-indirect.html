4. direct dan indirect dari kalimat diatas tlong ya.....?


Subject : English
Category : Reported Speech

Yang perlu diingat saat membuat kalimat langsung menjadi kalimat tidak langsung :
1. Jika reporting verb dalam past tense, tense nya mundur. Present jadi Past, Past jadi Past Perfect.
2. I adalah yang bicara, YOU adalah yang diajak bicara.
3. Perubahan keterangan waktu.
4. Kalimat positif dimulai dengan (THAT). Kalimat tanya dimulai dengan IF/WHETHER (untuk yes-no question) dan KATA TANYA. Kalimat perintah dimulai dengan TO + infinitive.
5. This→that, these→those, here→there

ANSWERS :
1. The manager said to Santi that he employed her.
2. The teacher told the students that they had to collect the assignment on November 10.
3. Rudi said that his mother was leaving for Jakarta soon.
4. Sasha told Iman that he needed to plan the program carefully.
5. Narti said that the school was organizing a trip for the students of Year 10 only.

21. buatlah kalimat 10 direct dan 10 indirect




Direct Speech adalah kalimat yang diucapkan langsung dari si pembicara. Kalimat

tersebut tidak diubah atau ditambah

Contoh :

-Erfin said, “I am so happy”.

-They said, “We have watched football game”.

Indirect Speech adalah kalimat yang berasal dari kalimat langsung yang diceritakan

kembali dalam bentuk yang lain.

Contoh :

-Erfin said that he was so happy

-They said that they had watched football game.

Catatan :

Jika Kata Kerja dalam induk kalimat bentuknya adalah PRESENT dan PRESENT PERFECT

atau adanya suatu keterangan yang umum pada induk kalimat, maka tidak ada

perubahan waktu dalam Kalimat tidak langsung.

Contoh :

-She asks me, “Are you sleepy?” She asks me wether I am sleepy

-He has told us, “I am hungry.” He told us that he is hungry

-She told me,“the sun rises in the east”.

She told me that the sun rises in the east

Tetapi apabila Kata Kerja dalam kalimat bentuknya selain dari PRESENT dan

PRESENT

PERFECT maka terjadi perubahan waktu pada Kalimat tidak langsung.

Contoh :

-Simple Present menjadi Simple Past

a. He told me, “I go to work everyday.”

b. He told me that he went to work everyday.

-Present Continuous menjadi Past Continuous

a. She told me, “I am playing music now”.

b. She told me that she was playing music then.

-Present Perfect menjadi Past Perfect

a. They told me. “We have bought a car”.

b. They told me that they had bought a car.

-Simple Past menjadi Past Perfect

a. He told me, “I went to Bandung yesterday”.

b. He told me that he had gone to Bandung yesterday.

-Simple Future menjadi Future Past

a. He told me, “I will go to university next year”.

b. He told me that he would go to university the following year.

-Future Past menjadi Future Past Perfect

a. She told me, “I should go there if I were you.”

b. She told me that she would have gone there if she had been me.

Perubahan-perubahan MODAL, PREPOSITION dan Keterangan waktu dari kalimat

langsung menjadi kalimat tak langsung. :

Shall – Should See – saw Will – would

Is – was May – might Has/have – had

Can –could are – were Here – there

This – that Now – then Ago – before, Dsb.

23. tolong buatkan kalimat direct dan indirect


Tolong buatkan kalimat direct dan indirect!

1. Indirect Sentence: Riki Setyanto, one of the parents, said that he had registered his daughter for the enrollment at state vacational high school SMKN 47 Jakarta, but she then got rejected due to the minimum height policy applied by the state-run school.

DIRECT SENTENCE: Riki Setyanto said, one of the parents, said, "I registered my daughter for the enrollment at state vacational high school SMKN 47 Jakarta, but she now gets rejected due to the minimum height policy applied by the state-run school.

2. Direct Sentence: "First my daughter was rejected because of her height, and now due to technical issues, she can't register at any school. I just want to get her into a good school," he said.

INDIRECT SENTENCE: He said that first his daughter had been rejected because of her height, and then due to technical issues, she couldn't register at any school. He just wanted to get her into a good school.

3. Indirect Sentence: Nurainsyah Paransa, another parent, also said that she was unableto register her son at any state-run high school due to similar technical problems.

DIRECT SENTENCE: Nurainsyah Paransa, another parent, also said, "I am unable to register my son at any state-run high school due to similar technical problems".

4. Direct Sentence: "But the second school rejected him because it said that he had been accepted through the public admission phase. Since my son did not register at the first school, now he isn't registered anywhere," Anisyah said.

INDIRECT SENTENCE: Anisyah said but the second school rejected him because it had said that he had been accepted through the public admission phase. Since her son hadn't registered at the first school, then he wasn't registered anywhere.

MATERI tentang Direct and Indirect Speech

How to change Direct Speech into Indirect Speech?

Berikut adalah beberapa poin penting yang harus diingat.

Dalam indirect speech tidak ada tanda petik (")Perhatikan perubahan pronoun (kata ganti) dalam indirect speechUbah tenses menjadi tenses yang lebih lampau. (Ini berlaku jika kita melaporkan sesuatu di lain hari, lain bulan, bahkan lain tahun, dan sudah tidak sesuai kenyataan sekarang)Ubah keterangan waktu dan tempat.Perubahan keterangan waktu dan tempat

Direct Speech ---> Indirect Speech

today ---> that day

now ---> then

tonight ---> that night

yesterday ---> the day before

two days ago ---> tow days before

last week ---> the previous week

next month ---> the following month

tomorrow ---> the next day/ the following day

here ---> there

this, these ---> that, those

Perubahan MODAL

Direct Speech ---> Indirect Speech

can, will, shall, may ---> could, would, should, might

must or have to ---> had to

* Modal should, should, might dalam direct speech TIDAK memerlukan perubahan dalam indirect speech.

* Untuk direct speech yang berupa pertanyaan interogative, sisipkan kata if/whether.

Contoh Direct - Indirect Speech

1. He said, “I am a teacher”

He said (that) he was a teacher

2. She said, “He is watching TV now”

She said (that) he was watching TV then.

3. He said, “They got an accident yesterday”

He said (that) they had got an accident the day before.

4. They said, “We were studying English”

They said (that) they had been studying English.

5. He said, “I have finished my homework”

He said (that) he had finished his homework.

6. He said, “I will buy a new motorcycle”

He said he would buy a new motorcycle.

7. She said, “I must visit my grandma”

She said (that) she had to visit her grandma.

8. She said, “Put off your shoes!”

She told me to put off my shoes.

9. She said, “Don’t be so nervous!”

She advised me not to be nervous.

10. She asked, “What did you do last night?"

She asked me why I had done that.
