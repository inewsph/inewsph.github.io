1. expression of giving instruction dengan contohnya


Expression:
First, ...
Next, ...
Then, ...
I would start by ...

Example:

I would like to make a hot coffee. First, I boil the water, then I pour the coffe into the glass. I dont like sweet, so I dont need to give a sugar. Next, I pour the boiled water into the glass then stir it. Hot coffee os ready to serve.

3. Pengertian tentang expression of asking and giving instruction


Expression of asking instruction = Ungkapan meminta instruksi atau petunjuk.
Contoh : Can you show me how to get the nearest hospital?
Expression of giving instruction = Ungkapan memberi arah atau petunjuk.
Contoh : Please go straight and then turn left. The hospital is beside the bank.

18. buat dialog untuk 4 orang tentang expression of capability, possibility, and giving instruction!


A: I want to make surprise dinner for my parents to celebrate their 16th wedding anniversary. What do you think?
C: I think that's a great idea. Your parents will be proud and happy to taste your food.
A: Starting from today, I want to practice my cooking skill so I can serve them with the best homemade food that taste good like a restaurant. Do you think it is possible for me to make it? (Ask capability)
D: Sure, it is possible for you to make it (possibility). The more you practice, the better you will become.
A: I have already prepared some ingredients here but I have no idea what to cook for dinner.
C: I think a beef steak is the best menu for dinner. It is also easy to make.
A: Do you know how to make it, C? (Asking capability)
C: I’m not sure I’m capable of doing it. You should ask B and D. They are really good at cooking.
B: Yes, I do (respond capability). You don't need to worry. I can teach you how to make simple and delicious steak that taste delicious just like my restaurant.
D: I'm not good as B, but I will be glad to help you. B is really good at cooking besides he is used to help his parents in the restaurant.
C: I'm not good at cooking but I can help you to cut some vegetables, boil water or something else.
A: I trust both of you. I know B have years of experience with cooking. D always makes delicious lunch by himself and brings it at school everyday. What kind of ingredients should I prepare, chefs?
D: Of course, the most important ingredient is a beef meat. It wil be good if you have tenderloin because it has tender texture.
A: How about seasoning or other ingredients?
B: You can use salt, black pepper, soy sauce, onion and garlic as seasoning. For other ingredients, you should prepare broth, you can use any kind of broth, butter and corn starch.
A: What is the first step?
D: Marinate the meat with pinch of baking soda and let it rest for 30 minutes (giving instruction). It helps to tenderize the meat. While waiting the meat, you can mince garlic & onion.
A: Okay, I understand. What is the second step?
B: Second step is making a brown sauce for the steak. You should melt butter in a pan to saute garlic & onion until golden brown. Then, pour stock in the pan. After that, add black pepper, soy sauce and corn starch until it boils (giving instruction).
C: Should I rinse the meat?
B: Yes, you should. You have to rinse the meat thoroughly to remove all the baking soda. The third step, sprinkle salt and pepper evenly over the meat. Melt the butter again and put your meat in the pan (giving instruction). You should cook it for about 3 minutes for each side. Remove steak from the pan and cover it loosely with foil. Let it stand for 10 minutes to keep juicy texture from the meat.
D: The last step, pour your steak with brown sauce (giving instruction). You can garnish your beef steak with boiled carrot, corn piles and peas. Your steak is ready to serve.
A: Thank you for helping me. There's a chance I can make delicious steak for my parents (possibility).

29. dialog gabungan antara expression making complain,suggestion,request, dan giving instruction


Kelas: SMA
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: Suggestion, making complaint, giving instructions, request
Kata kunci: suggestion, complaint, request, instruction, dialogue
Pembahasan:

Nancy: Excuse me, Miss.I'd like to file a complaint about my class. (complaint)
Receptionist: I see, what seems to be the problem with your class?
N: I think it's too noisy, miss. There are too many boys in there. I can't concentrate well. So, do you think you could move me to the other class with the same level? (request)
R: Let me see, well, we do have one but it's rather late. It starts at 6.30 pm. Would that be fine for you?
N: Oh, OK, but I won't be able to come every Friday because I have another course. So, I can only come on Wednesday only. Can you help me catching up with the lesson?
R: Well, we certainly can ask the teacher to help you with that by tutoring session. Shall we book that session for you every saturday morning? (suggestion)
N: Yeah, that's a good idea.
R: OK, then, hold on. Let me get the document first upstairs. (to office boy) Fetch me the tutoring session document now, please! (instruction)
Office Boy: sure, Ma'am.
