Jawaban:

contoh kalimat

Penjelasan:

should merupakan salah satu modal auxiliary yang artinya sebaiknya atau seharusnya berarti ini mengarahkan kepada rekomandasi atau Saran

misalnya kamu sakit lalu temanmu mengatakan bahwa kamu sebaiknya ke dokter : you should go to the doctor

rumusnya yaitu

kalimat positif (+)

kalimat negatif (-)

kalimat pertanyaan (?)

(+) subject+ should+ verb 1 + object

(-) subject + should + not+verb1+ object

(?) should+subject+verb1+ object+?.

verb 1 adalah kata kerja bentuk pertama misalnya borrow, play,go,sleep.

13. 1. Contoh 5 kalimat yang menggunakan kata "should" 2. Contoh 5 kalimat yang menggunakan kata "shouldn't"


kelas smp
Pelajaran bahasa inggris
kategori criticizing and advice
kata kunci should and shouldn't
pembahasan

One function of 'should' is for criticising
Another function of 'should' is for saying advice.
Example:
(should  - for criticising)
- The students should be studying now.
- The students should be respecting the teachers more.
- The students should be helping each other.
(should - for advice)
- The teacher should be more friendly.
- The teacher should give more homework to the sudents.

(shouldn't - for criticising)
- The students shouldn't be bullying their friends.
- The students shouldn't be playing around in the class.
(shouldn't - for advice)
- The teacher shouldn't throw stuff at the students.
- The teacher shouldn't ask some money to the students.
- The teacher shouldn't leave the problems behind without solution.

16. buatlah 5 contoh kalimat keharusan menggunakan kata should dalam Bahasa Inggris dan artinya​


Jawaban:

Contoh kalimat keharusan menggunakan kata 'should':

1. We should return the book immediately. (Kita harus segera mengembalikan buku itu.)

2. You should talk to your parent. (Kamu harus berbicara dengan orang tuamu.)

3. We should do the test well. (Kita harus mengerjakan tes dengan baik.)

4. We should tell him the truth. (Kita harus mengatakan yang sebenarnya padanya.)

5. You should try this a piece of cake. (Kamu harus mencoba sepotong kue ini.)

Penjelasan:

'Should' adalah salah satu jenis Modal Auxiliary Verb yang berbentuk past tense dari kata shall. Dalam Bahasa Indonesia, kata 'should' berarti 'harus' atau 'sebaiknya'. Biasa digunakan untuk memberikan nasihat.

Positive (+):

Subject + should + Verb 1

We should return the book immediately.

Negative (-):

Subject + should not/shouldn't + Verb 1

We should not return the book immediately

Interrogative (?):

Should + Subject + Verb 1 + ?

Should we return the book immediately?
