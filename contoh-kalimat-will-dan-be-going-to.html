Kelas : VII
Pelajaran : Bahasa Inggris
Kategori : simple future tense
Kata kunci : to be + going + to + verb1

Pembahasan:
To be + going + to digunakan pada simple future tense, yang menyatakan sesuatu yang telah direncanakan sebelumnya.
Rumus simple future tense:
Subject + will + verb1 = digunakan untuk suatu tindakan yang dilakukan tanpa direncanakan
Subject + to be (am, are, is) + going to + verb 1 = digunakan untuk suatu tindakan yang dilakukan terencana.

Contoh kalimat simple future tense dengan to be + going + to:
Kalimat positive 
1) She is going to go to Bali next year.
2) My father is going to send this letter tomorrow.
3) We are going to play basketball in two days.

Kalimat negative
4) They are not going to come his party next week.
5) My mother is not going to buy at the market next Sunday morning.
6) I am not going to watch the match because I have the examination next week.

Kalimat interrogative
7) Is he going to visit his grandparents next holiday?
8) Are we going to do this project?
9) Are you going to cook chicken soup for our dinner?
10) Is Via going to clean her room tomorrow morning?

To be + going to + verb1 juga dapat digunakan pada past future tense
(+) She was going to give this present to her sister.
(-) They were not going to enroll the English course.
(?) Was he going to buy a new car?

21. Contoh kalimat tenses be going to present (+)(-)(?) dan be going to past (+)(-)(?)...


*Present
pattern:
(+) = S + V1(s/es) + O(Adj./Adv/etc.)
(-) = S + Do/Does + not + V1 + O
(?) = Do/Does + S + V1 + O ?
Example :
(+) = He buys a car now
(-) = He does not buy a car now
(?) = Does he buy a car now ?

Note: keterangan waktu tidak harus ditulis.

*Past
pattern:
(+) = S + V2 + O
(-) = S + Did + not + V1 + O
(?) = Did + S + V1 + O ?
Example:
(+) = She went to school yesterday
(-) = She did not go to school yesterday
(?) = Did she go to school yesterday ?

Note: keterangan waktu tidak harus ditulis

26. Cari perbedaan going to and will be beserta penjelasannya dan contoh kalimatnya!Tolong bantu dong


Going to
Biasa digunakan saat seseorang akan melakukan hal tersebut dengan segera
atau pada saat yang bertepatan sebelum seseorang melakukan hal tersebut.

Contoh :
1. I'm going to accompany my mom to the mall. 
2. A : when will you do your homework? || B: I'm going to do it now.
3. She's going to the market by herself.

Will be
Biasa digunakan untuk seseorang yang akan melakukan atau menjadi sesuatu di masa depan namun waktunya tidak menentu/dalam waktu yang lama, namun juga bisa dalam jangka waktu yang cepat namun tidak segera

Contoh :
1. I will be a doctor.
2. my parents will accompany my brother at the graduation ceremony.
3. A : when will do your task? || B : I will do it this evening.
