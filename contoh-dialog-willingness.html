4. Contoh dialog yang memuat willingness dan capability untuk 3 orang


Contoh dialog yang memuat willingness dan capability untuk 3 orang adalah sebagai berikut.


Anna: Hey, guys, I will need your help.

Lulu: What is it Anna?

Anna: So I decided to move from the house that I rented to an apartment. The problem is, I have so many things that I don’t know whether I will be able to pack and transport all of them by myself.

Ika: That’s a piece of cake! Don’t worry Anna, you got us. I will be very willing to help you pack your stuff. What about you Lulu?

Lulu: Sure, me too! I am able to do it as long as we do it together.

Anna: Of course yes! I wouldn’t be able to do it alone either. Thanks a lot guys, I love you all!

Lulu & Ika: We love you too, Ann!


Pembahasan

Will dan Won't adalah istilah yang paling banyak digunakan dalam Bahasa Inggris untuk membicarakan tentang masa yang akan datang. Tetapi selain itu will dan won't memiliki fungsi yang lain, salah satunya untuk mengekspresikan keinginan atau ketidakinginan.


Expressing willingness, unwillingness:

will, won't, would, wouldn't


In the present: will, won’t

Kita dapat gunakan kata will untuk mengatakan bahwa seseorang ingin melakukan sesuatu:

Tom's a really kind person; he will always help you if you need somethingJosh will always be there to listen to your story, no matter how busy he is

Kita juga dapat gunakan kata will not/won't untuk mengatakan ketidakinginan untuk melakukan sesuatu:

David doesn't want to talk to me – he won't answer the phone.My car won't start - I'll have to take the bus

In the past: would, wouldn’t

Ketika berbicara dengan form lampau, kita dapat menggunakan would untuk menyatakan seseorang ingin melakukan sesuatu:

Tom was a really kind person; he'd (he would) always help anyone in any situation.Josh would love to come to your house on the weekend.

Menyatakan ketidakinginan untuk melakukan sesuatu di masa lampau:

My dad always helped with the cooking, but he wouldn't wash the dishes.I was late for work today because my car wouldn't start.

Ability adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk mengekspresikan bentuk kemampuan. Kata yang paling umum digunakan adalah can, could (past) dan managed to. Berikut ini adalah formula untuk menyatakan ability dalam bentuk present tense:


1. Is/am/are able to + main verb

Thomas is able to play the piano.Katy is not able to play the piano.

2. Can + main verb

Katy can play the flute.Thomas can't play the flute.Pelajari lebih lanjut soal-soal yang berhubungan tentang willingness and ability:

6. berikan contoh dialog will (willingness) yang agak panjang ya...


Before giving the dialogue samples, I think it's important to differentiate "will" and "willingness". While both are used in simple future, there's a slight difference between "will" and "willingness". We're going to discuss it on the discussion section.

Discussion

We usually use 'will+base verb' to express a future time in English. This pattern is also a formal way to of forming the future. We can use it when we're discussing our plans. For examples:

- I think I'll go to India next year.

- I will meet my teacher tomorrow to talk about the competition.

- I will go home after I finish reading this book.

This form can also be used to show our willingness to do something. It's often used when we're invited to do something or when we're asked to help someone with something.

For example:

(1) Invitation

A: Do you want to go to the mall later?

B: Sure, I'll go with you.

(2) Someone ask for help or our willingness

A: Will you help me with my homework?

B: Yeah... no problem. I'll help you with it during lunch break.

Doni: Will you forgive me?

Teresa: I will. I know you didn't mean to lose my book.

Answers

This dialogue is happening between a mother and her daughter at home. They're cleaning the house together because they're going to throw a small birthday party for the father. This dialogue shows the daughter willingness to help.

Mother: Nina, can you come here?

Nina: Okay, mom. Hold on. I'll come to the kitchen in a minute.

- 5 minutes later -

Nina: What is it? Do you need help?

Mother: Yes, can you wait here and check on the soup every 2 minutes? I have to call the cake shop and go to our neighbor's house to borrow a bigger rice cooker.

Nina: Sure, I'll do that.

Mother: Thanks.

- 15 minutes later -

Mother: Thanks, Nina.

Nina: Anything else I can help?

Mother: Oh, right. I ordered a cake from the cake shop near your school. They said it'll be finished by 4 pm tomorrow. But I don't think I can take it because I will need to prepare every thing before the party tomorrow. Will you go to school tomorrow?

Nina: Hmm... I'll go to school for club activity tomorrow. I think I can pick up the cake after I finish the practice, mom.

Mother: That's great!

Nina: Don't forget to remind me tomorrow.

Mother: I'll remind you tomorrow and I'll prepare the money too. I'll give it to you tonight.

8. Berikan contoh dialog the expression of willingness and unwillingness!


Berikut adalah contoh dialog yang berisikan expression of willingness and unwillingness.

Mr. Raimy: Hi Juan! How are you today?

Juan: Hello Mr. Raimy, I'm fine but a little tired today.

Mr. Raimy: Would you mind telling me what did you do today?

Juan: Yes I will, so this morning I did my school project as usual but then my parents asked me to help clean the house because there will be guests coming in the evening. We finished clean the house by noon and then had lunch. After that I continued doing my school project and now here I am.

Mr. Raimy: Wow you really had a busy day. Will you tell me about your school project that you worked on?

Juan: No, I won't. I am so sorry Mr. Raimy but I am so exhausted. Can I just lay down on your couch for 30 minutes before our practice?

Mr. Raimy: Of course, you can take a nap too if you will.

Juan: I will! Thank you so much Mr. Raimy.

Pembahasan

Expression of willingness and unwillingness memiliki makna secara harfiah yaitu ekspresi kesediaan dan keengganan. Maksud dari willingness adalah rasa kemauan/kerelaan untuk melakukan sesuatu. Sebaliknya unwillingness adalah rasa ketidakmauan seseorang untuk melakukan sesuatu. Untuk menyatakan ekspresi-ekspresi tersebut dalam bahasa Inggris dapat menggunakan modal auxiliary verb yaitu will dalam bentuk simple present atau menggunakan would dalam bentuk simple past. Berikut adalah aturan expression of willingness and unwillingness:

Untuk menyatakan willingnessS + will/would (like to) + V1 + O+ adv: I will take you to the doctor.S + to be + willing to + V1 + O + adv: She is willing to do the dishes later.Untuk menyatakan unwillingnessS + will/would + not + V1 + O + adv: Danny would not tell me about his girlfriend.Untuk menanyakan willingnessWill + S + V1 + O + adv + ?: Will you come tonight?Would + S + (like to) + V1 + O + adv + ?: Would Mary like to help in the kitchen?
