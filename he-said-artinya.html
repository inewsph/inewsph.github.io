Penjelasan:

Direct speech adalah kalimat langsung dalam bahasa Indonesia. Ketentuan penulisan kalimat langsung adalah adanya tanda koma sebelum kalimat yang diucapkan dan tanda petik dua.

Pada bahasa Inggris, indirect speech menggunakan past tense. Sehingga, pada direct speech menggunakan present tense.

Kalimat a, menggunakan simple past tense. Sehingga, direct speech-nya menggunakan simple present tense.

Kalimat b, menggunakan past perfect tense. Sehingga, direct speech-nya menggunakan simple past tense.

17. Change the sentence into direct speech.A. He said he lived in the city center.He said, "I ... " (simple present)B. He said he was going out.He said, "I ..." (present continuous)C. He said he had finished eating.He said, "I ..." (present perfect)D. He said he had been studying a lot.He said, "I ... " (present perfect continuous)​


Jawaban:

Direct & Indirect Speech

Mengubah bentuk menjadi Indirect Speech

A. He said, "I live in the city center."

B. He said, "I am going out."

C. He said, "I have finished eating."

D. He said, "I have been studying a lot."

Penjelasan:

• Direct & Indirect speech merupakan pola kalimat dalam bahasa Inggris. Direct speech adalah kalimat langsung atau disebut juga reported speech sedangkan Indirect speech merupakan kalimat tidak langsung atau laporan dari orang lain.

• Direct & Indirect speech memiliki pola kalimat berbeda disetiap bentuk waktunya (tenses).

• Untuk soal diatas :

A. Perubahan dari Simple Past (s+v2) menjadi Simple Present (s+v1).

- He said he lived in the city center.

- He said, "I live in the city center."

B. Perubahan dari Past Continuous (s+was/were+v+ing) menjadi Present Continuous (s+to be+ v1+ing).

- He said he was going out.

- He said, "I am going out."

C. Perubahan dari Past Perfect (s+had+v3) menjadi Present Perfect (s+have+v3).

- He said he had finished eating.

- He said, "I have finished eating."

D. Perubahan dari Past Perfect Continuous (s+had+been+v1+ing) menjadi Present Perfect Continuous (s+have+been+v1+ing).

- He said he had been studying a lot.

- He said, "I have been studying a lot."

• Terjemahan jawaban:

A. Dia berkata, "Saya tinggal di pusat kota".

B. Dia berkata, "Saya sedang keluar".

C. Dia berkata, "Saya sudah selesai makan".

D. Dia berkata, "Saya sudah belajar dengan giat".
