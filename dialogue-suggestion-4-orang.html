28. "make a dialogue consist of asking suggestion, giving suggestion, accept suggestion and refuse suggestion in 5 pairs"can you help me​


Jawaban:

Explanation:

Before making a dialog which consists of expression of suggestion, we should understand about expression of suggestion first to ease us in the process of making the dialog.

1. Asking suggestion is a type of expression which is used when someone asks for an advice or recommendation from another person.

Here are some expressions that you can use to ask for a suggestion:

- Could you give me an advice?

- What should ...?

- Any idea?

- Do you have any suggestions?

- What do you recommend ...?

2. Giving suggestion is a type of expression which is used when you want to give an advice or recommendation for someone.

Here are some expressions that you can use:

- You should ...

- You ought to ...

- It's better for you to ...

- I suggest you ...

- I advise you to ...

3. Respoding a suggestion

Responding a suggestion is divided into two, they are accepting and refusing.

a. Accepting a suggestion

Here are some expressions that you can use to accept a suggestion:

- I think so.

- It sounds a good idea.

- I agree with you.

- Alright, thank you.

- I will try it.

b. Refusing a suggestion

Here are some expressions that you can use to refuse a suggestion:

- I don't think so.

- I get your point, but ...

- I disagree with you.

- That's not a good idea.

Here is the dialog consisting the expression of suggestion

A: Hey, B!

B: Hey, A.

A: What's wrong? you don't look well.

B: I have a problem

A: Oh, would you like to tell your problem to me? I may can help you

B: Umm... I would like too but I don't want to mention his name.

A: It's alright.

B: Well, i have had an argument about the class project with one of the boy in the class.

A: and then?

B: He and i had a different opinion about the project. So we made a voting. My opinion was selected. But, It seemed like he really didn't like my opinion because he suddenly left the class without saying anything after my opinion won the voting.

A: I see.

B: Could you give me an advice?

A: Well, from your story i conclude that he is a stubborn boy, right?

B: Yes.

A: If that so, It's better for you to give in and accept his opinion.

B: I don't think so. The opinion has been accepted by the whole class.

A: But this is a class project, everyone is needed to make it, right? The other way you can do is persuading him. But persuading a stubborn person is so difficult.

B: You're right.

A: I advise you to discuss it with your classmates first. It's not something that you can decide alone.

B: I agree with you. Thank you, A!

A: Don't mention it, buddy.

Offer :

Student A : "Hi, there!"

Student B : "Hi! Can you help me?"

Student A : "Okay, what do you want?"

Student B : "I want to read this book, but I want that book too. Can you choose for me?"

Student A : "Maybe you can read romantic book"

Student B : "What the title of the book?"

Student A : "Romeo and Juliet"

Student B : "Okay, thank you so much!"

Pembahasan

Suggestion adalah bahasa Inggris dari saran. Saran adalah sebuah hal yang dapat menjadi sebuah solusi permasalahan yang sedang dialami. Namun, tidak semua saran dilakukan. Jika saran tersebut tidak membangun dan tidak objektif, maka hal tersebut saran melainkan mengkritik. Objektif adalah sebuah sifat dimana tidak melibatkan perasaan pribadi.

Offer adalah bahasa Inggris dari menawarkan. Menawarkan adalah sebuah hal yang mengunjukkan sesuatu agar dipilih oleh sang penerima tawaran. Misalnya, rumah. Rumah yang dikontrak atau disewa merupakan bagian dari tawaran. Sang penawar sengaja membuatnya agar sang penerima tawaran tertarik dan langsung menghubungi sang penawar untuk menuju proses lebih lanjut
