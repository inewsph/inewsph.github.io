21. Ungkapan Penting dalam Percakapan Permohonan Maaf (Apologizing)...? ​


Ungkapan Penting dalam Percakapan Permohonan Maaf (Apologizing)...?

Preliminary

To express regret for a mistake or apologize.

Expressions Of Apologizing

SorryI'am sorryI'am so sorryI'am really sorrySorry it was may faultI do apologizeI apologizePardon mePlease accept my apologySorry for my mistakes

These words are used to apologize to someone we have done wrong.

Responses Of apologizing

That's all right.That's okay.It's all right.It's okay.It's fine.Forget it.Never mind.Don't apologize.It doesn't matter.Don't worry about it.

These words are used to answer from the word apologize. For example :

I'm sorry

Then, the answer to that word is

It's okay

Answer

Apologizing :

SorryI'am sorryI'am so sorryI'am really sorrySorry it was may faultI do apologizeI apologizePardon mePlease accept my apologySorry for my mistakes

Explanation

Apologizing, to apologizing means apologizing for mistakes/mistakes to others. In other words, apologizing can mean regretting what he did.

Expression of Apologizing is a way of conveying expressions of apology in English which of course has many variations of sentences according to the context and situation.

Apology is an expression of regret or remorse for an action, whereas apologizing is an act of expressing regret or regret. In informal situations, it can be called apologizing.

Example of a formal apology :

Apologizing can be divided into formal and informal and followed by the response of the apology.

Contoh permintaan maaf yang formal:

Please forgive me. I am very/terribly/awfully sorry. I am sorry for the trouble I have caused you. I am sorry for disturbing you. I am sorry that I made you wait for me.I apologize for yesterday. Please accept my apologies. Please forgive my rudeness. I must apologize for my mistakes. It’s all my fault. I’m ashamed to admit that I made an error.I have to admit that I am not good enough. I beg your pardon.There has been some misunderstanding.Sorry for the misunderstanding.

Example of a non-formal apology :

Sorry I’m late. It’s my fault. My bad.I messed/screwed up. Please don’t be angry/mad at me. I didn’t mean it/that. Sorry, didn’t mean to hurt your feeling. I am telling you, I am sorry.Sorry, it was an accident.Please don’t take it personally.

26. Buatlah contoh percakapan greeting, leave taking, apologizing, dan thangking dengan jumlah 4 orang dalam satu percakapan!


Dialog Greetings

Roy : “Good morning, Sir. My name is Roy..”

Mr. Adi : “Good morning, Roy. How can I help you?.”

Roy : “I am a new student and I do not know where my class is.”

Mr. Adi : “Alright. What class are you in?”

Roy : “7.G class, Sir.”

Dialog Greetings 2

Jennie : “Hi, Ben!”

Ben : “Hi, Jennie. What is up? It has been ages since we met last time.”

Jennie : “I am good. What about you?”

Ben : “Well, nothing special. I have just finished my school and found someone special.”

Jennie : “Oh my God! Congratulations. I am so happy for you.”

Dialog leave taking

Billy : “Excuse me. I think we have met before.”

Ella : “oh, right. Are you Billy the owner of the bakery?”

Billy : “Yes, I am. Are you waiting for the train?

Ella : “Yes, I am. Are you?”

Billy : “Yeah, me too. Oh there, that is my train. I have to go. Nice to meet you, Ella.”

Ella : “Nice to meet you too, Billy.”

Dialog Apologizing

Mr. Adnan : “Linda, have you put the weekly report to my desk?”

Linda : “I am sorry, Sir. I lost the weekly report.”

Mr. Adnan : “Are you sure you lost it?”

Linda : “Yes, sir. I have looked for it everywhere but I could find it. I am terribly sorry for

my mistake, Sir. I will write the new one as soon as possible.”

Mr. Adnan : “Okay, but please don’t let it happen again.”

Dialog Thangking

Adel : “Miss, you left your phone in the class. Here is it.”

Miss Selsi : “Oh, I think I lost it. Thank you for giving it back to me, Adel.”

Adel : “You are welcome, Miss.”
