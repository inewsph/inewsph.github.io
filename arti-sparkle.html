This type of text comes in many forms, such as instruction manuals and recipes. The text consists of three parts:Title/ Goal : It states the goal to be achieved.List of materials : It lists the materials needed. It often gives detail on the size, color,numbers,shapes,quantity, etc,List of equipments : It lists the equipments needed. It also often gives detail on the size,color, numbers, shapes, quantity, etc.Steps/Method/ : It describes steps in logical order to achieve the goal.Procedure : The steps are often marked with numbers ( 1,2,3...), letter ( a,b,c...), or bullet marks (*, >, tanda ini kasih balik yah &, dll ). Sometimes the steps include caution (s) or warning (s). There are also drawings or pictures to make the steps clear.
