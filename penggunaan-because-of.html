8. Perbedaan penggunaan because of dan due to


Jawaban: Due to is used if the sentence followed after that is a noun, meanwhile; because of should be followed with a verb after that.

Penjelasan: Take a look on the example below

Due to = My friends' party was canceled due to the terrible accident happened recently near her house (here, "terrible accident" word act as a noun)

Because of = I thought her party was canceled because of that accident. (meanwhile, in this part "that accident", it is used to emphasized that because of the accident the party is canceled)

13. penggunaan because , because of or due to


Kelas: SMP
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: connectors
Pembahasan: 

The use of 'because' is to state a cause-and-effect sentence followed by a phrase or a sentence.
Example: She refused to go out with Vitor because he had a scandalous past life.
The use of 'because of' and 'due to' is to state a cause-and-effect sentence followed by a noun
Example: She refused to go out with Vitor because of / due to his scandalous past life.

14. buat kalimat menggunakan kata because,because of ,due to


Ini rumus singkat dan contoh kalimat untuk kata because, because of, dan due to 

Because + S + V 
contoh:
The teacher didn't come because she is sick 
She fell down because she doesn't walk carefully 

Because of + Noun 
contoh: 
We stayed home because of the cold weather 
Because of my parents' generosity all of the children in our family have recieved the best of everything. 


Due to + Noun 
contoh: 
We were late to the meeting due to the heavy traffic 
Due to bad weather, we can't go surfing.

25. She did not buy the bag because it was expensive menggunakan kalimat because and because of


Answer :

In the clause of reason, ,you have several words to use, because / for / since / as, which is followed by sentence and due to / because of, which is followed by noun / gerund.
From the sentence above :
She didn't buy the bag because it was expensive.
If using 'because of ', it will be
She didn't buy the bag because of the expensive price. OR
she didn't buy the bag because of the fact that it was expensive.

Kelas :SMP
Mapel : English
Kategori : clause of reason

29. contoh kalimat menggunakan kata because dan because of (masing-masing 5) ​


JAWAB:

Cause and Effect

contoh kalimat menggunakan kata because dan because of (masing-masing 5)

The sentence structure of cause and effect

Because of + noun Because + subject + verb

Because

I can't do this because I don't understand how to do it.She didn't come to the party last night because her mum forbade her to come.I get a punishment from my teacher because I don't do my homeworkShe went to the dentist because she got toothache.We decided to cancel our trip because we didn't have enough money.

Because of

Because of his hard work, he managed to get the best student awardShe becomes a very famous artist because of her beauty The flight was cancelled because of the bad weatherBecause of your love, I can feel happiness.Because of his help, my father can run his business well

PEMBAHASAN:

Pengertian Cause and Effect

Cause adalah:

The reason or motive, for an action Why something happen Happen first

Effect adalah:

Result or conclusion of an action What happened Happens due to a cause

Jadi cause and effect biasa juga disebut dengan kalimat sebab-akibat atau kalimat kausalitas adalah kalimat yang dibangun dengan melibatkan tindakan yang membuat sesuatu terjadi dan hasil dari tindakan tersebut. Paling gampang ditandai dengan kata hubung yang menandakan sebab akibat.

Signal words of cause

Ketika kita bicara tentang cause maka kata- kata yang dapat kita gunakan adalah sebagai berikut:

Because  the reason for  On account of  Bring about  Give rise to  Created by  Contributed to  Led to  Due to  For this reason  Unless

Signal words of Effect

Ketika kita bicara tentang effect maka kata- kata yang dapat kita gunakan adalah sebagai berikut:

As a result  Then  Hence  For this reason Outcome  So  Consequently  Finally  Therefore  In order to

The sentence structure of cause and effect

Due to/Because of/owing to/Thanks to + noun Because/Since/As/For/As a result of + subject + verb

Owing to her hard work and intelligence, we won the trophy. Thanks to Ammar and Ameera’s effective planning, the event went well I have  a stomach ache because I ate too much food There was a lot of homework  and test, as a result most of the students were unhappy and couldn’t go anywhere during the weekend.  I don’t go to school because I am sick I am sleepy, so I don’t go to school He can not drive as he does not have license Since he was sick, the teacher allowed her to stay at home Now Ammar lives far from his family that he can be an independent boy Now Amira is sick that she can't go to school.
