21. Write down the pattern of Simple Past Tense in Positive, Negative, Interrogative!(-)​


The pattern of simple in positive, negative and interrogative is..

(+) S + Verb-2 + O

(-) S + Did + not + Verb-1 + O

(?) Did + S + Verb-1 + O

PEMBAHASAN

Sebelum kita membahas pattern of simple past (rumus dari simple past), mari kita pahami terlebih dahulu fungsi dari simple past itu sendiri.

Simple Past

↪ Berfungsi untuk menjelaskan tentang kegiatan yang terjadi pada masa lampau.

Rumus :

(+) S + Verb-2 + O

They went to Jakarta last month. She washed her clothes yesterday.

(-) S + Did + not + Verb-1 + O

They did not go to Jakarta last month. She did not wash her clothes yesterday.

(?) Did + S + Verb-1 + O

Did they go to Jakarta last month? Did she wash her clothes yesterday?

➡ Perhatikan baik-baik setiap perubahan yang terjadi pada contoh diatas!!

Catatan :

✔ Verb-2 hanya digunakan pada kalimat positif (+).

✔ Auxiliary verb yang digunakan pada kalimat negatif (-) dan tanya (?) adalah did. Dan kata kerjanya berubah menjadi verb-1.

Untuk mengetahui perubahan verb-2 dari suatu kata kerja, kita bisa melihat perubahannya dari daftar regular verbs (kata kerja beraturan) atau irregular verbs (kata kerja tidak beraturan).

30. CARILAH CONTOH KALIMAT SIMPLE PAST TENSE (POSITIVE , NEGATIVE , DAN INTERROGATIVE) DARI KATA A.LEAVE B.FEEL C.WRITE D.GO


║SIMPLE PAST TENSE-AFFIRMATIVE-NEGATIVE-INTERROGATIVE║

[tex]Answer[/tex]

Ciri khas utama penerapan Simple past tense adalah bentuk kata kerja utamanya dengan menggunakan kata kerja kedua/ preterite.

1. (+) She left her pencil case at class yesterday. ( leave=>left )

(-) She didn't leave her pencil case at class yesterday.

(?) Did she leave her pencil case at class yesterday?

2. (+) He felt happy when he got a present from his parents. (feel=>felt)

(-) He didn't feel happy when he got a present from his parents.

(?) Did he feel happy when he got a present from his parents?

3. (+) My sister wrote a letter yesterday. ( write=> wrote )

(-) My sister didn't write a letter yesterday

(?) Did my sister write a letter yesterday?

4. (+) George went to the beach two days ago. ( go=> went )

(-)George didn't go to the beach two days ago.

(?) Did George go to the beach two days ago?

Berdasarkan pertanyaan yang diajukan, kata-kata "leave, feel, write, dan go" merupakan bentuk kata kerja baik berupa dynamic verb maupun stative verb. Maka, hal tersebut dikategorikan sebagai kalimat verbal.

Untuk mengubah ke bentuk affirmative, negative & interrogative di dasarkan pada pola berikut ini :

(+)  S+verb 2 + O/ C

(-)   S+did+not+verb 1 + O/C

(?)  Did+S+verb 1+O/C  ?

[tex]Explanation[/tex]

Simple past tense mendeskripsikan tindakan yang telah dimulai dan diselesaikan juga pada waktu tertentu di masa lampau . Durasi waktu kegiatan tidak ditentukan, bisa saja baru - baru ini telah terjadi dan kejadian yang memang sudah lama terjadi di masa lampau.

Berdasarkan struktur kalimatnya, dibagi 2 jenis yaitu verbal sentence & Nominal sentence .

1. Verbal sentence

merupakan struktur kalimat yang predikatnya berupa kata kerja. Contoh : eat, run, drink, do, walk, read, watch, swim and etc.

Pattern of Verbal Sentence

(+) Subject + Verb 2 + Object/Complement  

(-) Subject + did + not + verb 1 + Object/ Complement

(?) Did+Subject+Verb 1 + Object/Complement ?

2.Nominal sentence

memiliki struktur kalimat dimana predikatnya bukan berupa kata kerja. Pada umumnya diikuti kata benda (noun), kata sifat (adjective) dan keterangan ( adverb ).

Pattern of nominal sentence :

(+) Subject + tobe ( was/were ) + noun/adjective/adverb

(-) Subject + tobe(was/were) + not + noun/adjective/adverb  

(?) tobe(was/were)+Subject + noun/adjective/adverb?

ADVERB OF TIME

( Keterangan waktu identifikasi bentuk simple past)

1. Definite point in time ( waktu penentu jelas )

→ last week, yesterday, two years ago, last night, this morning

Example :

Verbal

(+ ) She  met her friend this morning.

( - ) She did not meet her friend this morning.

( ? ) Did she meet her friend this morning?

Nominal

(+ ) I was in Paris last year.

( - ) I was not in Paris last year.

(? ) Was I in Paris last year  ?

2. An indefinite point in time ( dalam waktu yang tidak ditentukan )

→ a long time ago, the other day

Example :

Verbal  

(+ ) He lived with his grandparents  when he was young.    

(- )  He did not live with his grandparents  when he was young.

( ? ) Did he live with his grandparents when he was young?

Nominal

(+) He was an army  a long time ago      

(- ) He wasn't an army a  long time ago

( ? ) Was he an army a long time ago ?
