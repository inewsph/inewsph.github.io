9. Buatlah Contoh Short message, minimal 5 berserta artinya!​


1. Dear Ratna,

Hi, where are you? I came to your class to return your pen just now, but you were not in. So I put your pencil on your table.

Thanks for it.

Bella.

⟩⟩ artinya =

Untuk Ratna,

Hai, kamu dimana? Aku ke kelasmu untuk mengembalikan pulpen mu, tapi kamu tidak ada dikelas. Jadi aku meletakkannya diatas mejamu.

Terimakasih ya.

Bella.

2. To : Beti.

I’m sorry, I can’t come to your house to do our English task this afternoon. I must take my mother to the doctor because she has a headache. What if we do it tomorrow?

Dona.

⟩⟩ artinya =

Kepada : Beti.

Maafkan aku, aku tidak bisa kerumahmu untuk mengerjakan tugas Bahasa Inggris siang ini. Aku harus mengantar ibuku ke dokter karena dia sakit kepala. Bagaimana jika kita mengerjakannya besok?

Dona.

3. Dear Anisa.

I have an essay assignment about reusing thing. Could you please accompany me to go to the library to look up resources? The assignment should be submitted by the end of this week. Can we go there after school? Reply soon, please.

Yulia.

⟩⟩ artinya =

Untuk Anisa.

Aku punya tugas esai tentang penggunaan barang bekas. Dapatkah kamu menemaniku untuk pergi ke perpustakaan untuk mencari buku sumbernya? Tugasnya harus dikumpulkan akhir pekan ini. Dapatkah kita pergi kesana setelah pulang sekolah? Tolong balas secepatnya.

Yulia.

4. Hi, Ayu!

By the way, we have a project in biology class. It’s about cassava fermentation. We are in the same team, right? How about do it next two days in my home?

Bianka.

⟩⟩ artinya =

Hai, Ayu!

Ngomong-ngomong, kita punya tugas di pelajaran biologi. Ini tentang fermentasi singkong. Kita dalam satu kelompok yang sama, kan? Bagaimana jika kita mengerjakannya dua hari lagi dirumahku?

Bianka.

5. Dear Fika,

Remember, we’d had an appointment to go swimming after school. I’ll be waiting for you at home. Don’t forget to bring money. See you!

Nandita.

⟩⟩ artinya =

Untuk Fika,

Ingat, kita punya janji untuk berenang setelah pulang sekolah. Aku akan menunggumu dirumah. Jangan lupa membawa uang. Sampai ketemu lagi!

Nandita.

11. Tolong buatkan short messege beserta balasannya dan beserta pertanyaan pertanyaan dari short message tersebut yang jumlahnya ada 10


Dear, Lucy. I'm sorry I can't come to your house to bake cheesecake together. My car is broken and I have to go repair it because I have to use it tonight to pick my dad from the airport.
-Anna

Dear Anna, it doesn't matter. I already bought the ingredients, but it's okay. I can keep it and we can do it next Sunday. I wish you the best.
- Lucy

1. Who's sending the message first?
2. Why Lucy can't come to Anna's house?
3. Why does Anna have to repair her car as soon as possible?
4. When does Anna have to pick his dad?
5. Where does Anna have to pick his dad?
6. What are they going to do at Anna's house?
7. Who has the ingredients to bake the cheescake?
8. Does Anna get upset because Lucy can't come to her house?
9. Do they have another plan to bake the cheesecake?
10. If yes, when is that?

Penjelasan:

Notice adalah informasi singkat dan sederhana yang ditujukan kepada publik. Notice harus mudah dipahami dan mudah dibaca, sehingga biasanya Notice atau pemberitahuan selalu menggunakan kata-kata yang sederhana, ditulis dengan jenis huruf (font) yang sederhana, dan huruf berukuran besar. Biasanya diletakkan di tempat-tempat umum. Notice juga bisa berbentuk rambu-rambu.

Short message (Pesan pendek) adalah pesan yang dibuat secara singkat untuk menyampaikan sesuatu kepada orang lain agar orang tersebut melakukan atau tidak melakukan sesuatu.

Announce

adalah pengumuman atau pemberitahuan.


19. apa itu short message


short message artinya pesan pendek atau pesan singkatShort Message dalam Bahasa Indonesia artinya adala Pesan Pendek atau Pesan Singkat. Short Message dibuat dengan berbagai urutan :
1. Address (alamat pengirim)
2. Date (tanggan surat ditulis)
3. Salutation (salam pembuka)
4. Body of the letter (isi surat)
•Opening / introduction
•Content ( tentang surat)
•Closing (penutup
5. Complementery Close (salam penutup)
6. Name of sender (nama pengirim)

27. TULOOOOOONGGGG....... MEMBUAT SHORT MESSAGE / PESAN PENDEK ( 2 PESAN)​


Jawaban:

~ Short Message

A short message is used to send am important message to other people , friend or Family . A short message should be :

1). Clear to whom the addressee is

2). Straight forward

3). State clearly and easy to understand

EXAMPLE :

1.

Dear Sari ,

Tomorrow I plan to come to your house to study Math with you at 4 am . Will you be at home ?

See you,

Diana

2.

Dear Mrs. Judd,

I'm sorry , I have to Miss your lesson today . My mother has a doctor's appoinment and I have to go home and look after my little sister . I'll get the important notes and homework Topic from one of my classmates tomorrow.

Kitty


28. cari dari short message dan notice a.definisi short messsage dan notice b. fungsi short message dan notice c.struktur teks dari short message dan notice d. cari masing masing 5 contoh dari short message dan notice


a. notice is to give instruction to someone.

short message is a written piece that you send or leave to another person

b. notice functioned as an instruction to someone about things to do or not to do

short message structure : addresses/receiver, content, sender
