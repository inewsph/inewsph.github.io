Here is one personal letter based on the situation on number 1:

Dear Ximena,

How have you been? I haven't heard anything from for a while. It seems that you have been inactive in social media for the past few months. Well, I hope everything is alright with you. Anyway, my school is planning to have a study tour in Bali next month, ain't that great? we can finally meet again. What do you say? I will be there for two days and staying in Denpasar. I hope you can make the time for our meeting. I'm still carving on the Mexican food you made before our parting. Let me know soon, OK? I look forward to it.

Regards,

Aliou

Penjelasan:

When it comes to writing personal letters, which are mainly for someone close to us either friends, family and relatives, the format tends to be less strict than business letters.

Here are some requirements for personal letter:

using informal languageit can be long, short or even very long depending on the content.using first person or even terms of endearment when addressing to each other.using slang words, phrasal verbs are possible.
