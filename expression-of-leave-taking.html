5. crate a dialog use expression of leave taking​


Example of Leave Taking Dialogue

Gaffar: Hi, Putri!

Putri: Hi, Gaffar.

Gaffar: How are you doing?

Putri: Pretty well. By the way. Where are you going?

Gaffar: I’m going to the swimming pool. Will you join me?

Putri: Oh, sorry I must help my father in the studio.

Gaffar: It’s okay. See you next time.

Putri: See you. Have a nice day.

Explanation:

Leave taking or parting usually used if we part with someone we know.

example, the expression of greeting :

1. Ranti meets her teacher, Mr Bakri at the post office.

Ranti : Good afternoon, Sir.

Mr Bakri : Good afternoon. You are ....

Ranti : Ranti, Sir. My name is Ranti. How are you?

Mr Bakri : I am fine, thank you. And how about you?

Ranti : I am fine, too.

Mr Bakri : Well, Ranti. I have to go now. Pleased to meet you.

Ranti : Pleased to meet you, too, Sir.

2. Cipto waiting for Andi and Yani, his new colleague at the airport.

Andi : Excuse me. Are you Henri?

Cipto : Yes. I'm Cipto Susanto.

Andi : How do you do, Cipto? I'm Andi from University of Jakarta.

Cipto : How do you do, Cipto. Nice to see you.

Andi : Nice to meet you, too. Did you have a good journey?

Cipto : Yes. It was fine, thanks.

Andi : Let me help you to bring your suitcase.

Cipto : That's very kind of you.

Andi : Not at all. Let me introduce you to my friend Yani this is Cipto. Cipto this is Yani.

Yani : Hello, Cipto. Nice to meet you.

Cipto : Pleased to meet you, too. How's the trip?

Example, the expression of leave taking.  

Bobi : Hi Sindy, good morning.  

Sindy : Hi Bobi, good morning.  

Bobi : How are you, Sindy?  

Sindy : Yeah I’m great. How about you, how are the things?  

Bobi : Everything is fine. How’s your research?  

Sindy : It is going well. Thanks for asking. I’m still collecting some data now. How’s yours?    

Bobi : Glad to hear that. Mine is already 70%. There are some things that I don’t understand.  Maybe you can do me a favor please.                

Sindy : Sure, I will help you, Bobi.

Sindy : Thanks for your compliment. I just want to help.  

Bobi : Thank you, Sindy.  

Sindy : Okay Bob, just call me. I’m sorry I have to go now, Bobi. I forgot to buy a cake for  my brother’s birthday.  

Bobi : That’s okay, Sindy. I will call you later then. See you.  

Sindy : See you, Bob.
