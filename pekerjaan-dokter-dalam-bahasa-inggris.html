15. sebutkan 10 macam dokter dalam bahasa inggris beserta arti dan deskripsikan pekerjaan itu..!


10 macam dokter dalam bahasa Inggris adalah sebagai berikut.

1. Gynaecologist: Dokter kandungan

Job desc: Diagnoses, treats and helps prevent diseases of women related to reproductive system. They also provide medical care regarding to pregnancy.

2. Dermatologist: Dokter kulit

Job desc: Treats common skin problem such as sunburns, acne and rashes.

3. Cardiologist: Ahli jantung

Job desc: Diagnoses and treats patients with cardiovascular problems.

4. Dentist: Dokter gigi

Job desc: Diagnoses and treats related to dental issues.

5. Surgeon: Dokter bedah

Job desc: Treats diseases, injuries by invasive or non-invasive surgical methods.

6. Pediatrician: Dokter anak

Job desc: Treats and diagnoses infants, children and adolescents from illness/injuries.

7. General practitioner: Dokter umum

Job desc: Treats and diagnoses diseases which are commonly occur in majority of people.

8. Veterinarian: Dokter hewan

Job desc: Diagnoses and treats disease of pets and livestock.

9. Internist: Ahli penyakit dalam

Job desc: Diagnoses and provide treatment of diseases located in the internal organ systems.

10. Gastroenterologist: Gastrologi

Job desc: Specializes in treating illness/diseases related to digestive system.

22. deskripsi pekerjaan dokter dakam bahasa inggris


Writing Descriptive Text

Medical doctor or simply doctor is a professional who practices medicine and qualified to diagnose and treat people who are ill. Most doctors have specific expertise in one field of medicine or another.

Job description of medical doctors are to diagnose patient conditions using tests and examination. And based on the information they earned, doctors will prescribe treatment and medication to attempt to heal patient’s illnesses or injuries.

Pembahasan

Deskripsi tentang dokter di atas masuk dalam kategori descriptive text. Descriptive text adalah sebuah bacaan yang memberikan informasi mengenai suatu tempat, orang, binatang, dll. Pada descriptive text sifatnya lebih mengarah kepada penggambaran, pengungkapan secara detil dan penjelasan.

Generic structure dari descriptive text adalah:

Identification => Pendahuluan, yakni gambaran umum suatu topikDescription => Ciri-ciri tentang topik yang dibahas, sifat-sifat yang dimiliki, tampilan khas, dll.
