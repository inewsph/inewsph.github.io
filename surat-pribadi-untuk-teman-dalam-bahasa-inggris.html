In making correspondence online, you need to keep the register informal in that context as it's only for making penfriends, not for business situation. At the early stage of online friendship, try to get her/his trust, never ask too much and personal questions and keep email short, polite but regularly sent. So, you mustn't be in a rush in making friends online like that, no matter how gorgeous/ attractive she/he is .
