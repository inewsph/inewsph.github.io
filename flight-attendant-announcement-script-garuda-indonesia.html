21. 2. Mention the task and workplace for the following professions !a. Waiterb. Sales assistantc. Flight attendantd. Optician​


Answer:

a . WAITER

task : greet customers , take food orders , bring food and drinks to the tables and take payment and make change .

workplace : full—service restaurants

b . SALES ASSISTANT

task : liaising with customers in order to ensure a transaction goes smoothly . a sales assistant is expected to help customers choose between the company ' s array of goods and services , process payments and maintain a high level of customer service .

workplace : retail companies

c . FLIGHT ATTENDANT

task : ensure the safety and comfort of passengers during an airline flight .

workplace : airplane

d . OPTICIAN

task : design , verify , and fit eyeglass lenses and frames , contact lenses , and other devices to correct eyesight .

workplace : hospitals , laboratories , eye care centers or retail stores .
