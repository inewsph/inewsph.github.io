3. You work in a tour agency. You see a young gentleman enter your office awkwardly. Offer your service and try to convince him to take one of your holiday packages.

Employee: Is there anything I can help you with, Sir? We are currently still in the vibes of Thanksgiving, so the black friday discount still valid. The last day will be tomorrow.

Customer: Yes, I’m actually planning for a trip to Japan with my wife.

Employee: You may check this package out, sir. With the black friday discount you’ll get 30% off from the normal price. It’s a package for 10 days trip in the 4 stars hotel. The facility is very competitive comparing to other travel agencies since comfort is our priority.

Customer: Hmm.. I’m quite interested with this one.



4. A friend is absent because she is sick. You visit her this afternoon. Your friend needs your help to communicate with the teacher about an assignment that she hasn’t finished yet. Offer her a help.

You: Hey, Lala how do you feel?

Lala: I still need to have some bed rest the doctor said.

You: I see, I hope you’ll get better soon. Oh by the way, what about your English task?

Lala: That’s the problem. I still haven’t finished it and the deadline is tomorrow right? I’m so worried about it.

You: Don’t worry Lala. I will take care of it. Let me talk to Ms. Lidya about your current situation, and I’m sure she will understand.

Lala: Thanks a lot! You are truly the person I can rely on.

You: No worries, a friend in need, is a friend indeed.


5. You want to go to the movie this weekend. You ask several friends to go with you. Two of your friends cannot make up their minds. Offer to treat them so that they can go with you.

You: Hey guys, I have 3 free movie tickets. Would you like to join me?

Anna: What’s the movie?

You: It’s Insidious 3.

Luna: OMG, I hate horror!

Anna: I have watched it last week with my brother.

Luna: Hmmm sounds good. How about you Anna?
