8. rumus dari "would prefer... ratherthan.... "tolong dijawab ​


Jawaban:

Would prefer =“would prefer” digunakan dalam situasi yang lebih spesifik. Tentu saja sama seperti frasa preferensi yang lain, ada pola kalimat yang harus diikuti yaitu:

Would prefer + to infinite + rather than + infinite

Contohnya saat digunakan dalam kalimat:

I would prefer to order English articles for my niche blogs rather than write them.

President Joko Widodo would prefer to wear white shirt for his daily duties rather than wear coat.

Kadang kala, frasa dengan “would prefer” digunakan untuk situasi yang tidak benar-benar terjadi atau unreal.  

Simak tanya jawab berikut ini?

Would you rather marry Brad Pitt or Johnny Depp? I would rather marry Johnny Depp than Brad Pitt. I like his act!

Would you rather be a bird or a dolphin? I would rather be a bird than a dolphin because I don’t like fish.

Jika digunakan dalam kalimat negatif maka akan menjadi seperti:

Would you rather marry Brad Pitt or Johnny Depp? I would rather not marry either. I would rather marry David Beckham.

Would you rather be a bird or a dolphin? I would rather not be either. I would rather be a cat.

Ratherthan = Yang kedua, untuk mengungkapkan preferensi, kita bisa menggunakan frasa ‘rather than’. Untuk yang ini, kita perlu memadukan dengan frasa ‘would prefer’ di depannya. Adapun formulanya sebagai berikut:

Subject + Would Prefer + Noun/Gerund + Rather Than + Noun/Gerund

Sebab ‘rather than’ menyatakan perbandingan, maka jelas harus ada dua hal yang disebutkan. Jika diaplikasikan dalam kalimat, maka contohnya seperti di bawah ini:

I would prefer buying food rather than cooking (Saya lebih suka membeli makanan daripada memasak)

I would prefer pasta rather than pizza (Saya lebih suka pasta daripada pizza)

I would prefer staying here rather than living abroad (Saya lebih suka tinggal di sini daripada hidup di luar negeri)

Selain itu, kita juga dapat menggunakan to –infinitive untuk pilihan utama dan bare infinitive di pilihan selanjutnya, berlaku untuk kata kerja transitif maupun intransitif.

Contoh:

Anita would prefer to do the dishes rather than cook for the dinner

(Anita lebih suka/lebih memilih mencuci piring daripada memasak untuk makan malam)

Samuel would prefer to sing rather than dance for the party (Samuel lebih suka/lebih memilih menyanyi daripada menari di pesta itu)

Gunakan "to + verb" setelah kata prefer

Contoh : I prefer to eat healty food rather than junk food

Gunakan "verb" setelah kalimat would rather

Contoh : I would rather ('d rather) eat healty food than junk food

Dia kedua Noun nya cuma boleh diganti ama Infinitive Kalau "Would Rather" rumusnya S + would rather + noun + rather than + noun Would rather sama dengan prefer.. Would prefer + to + V1 + rather than/instead of
- I would prefer to walk to your house.
I would prefer to walk to your house rather than use a train.
- I would prefer to have a dog.
I would prefer to have a dog instead of a cat.
Would prefer + noun + rather than/instead of
- I would prefer cheesecake
I would prefer cheesecake rather than strawberry cake.
- I would prefer short pants
I would prefer short pants instead of skirt.

Would rather + bare infinitive (infinitive tanpa to)/V1 + than
- I would rather be dead.
I would rather be dead than live with you.
- I would rather sleep
I would rather sleep than watch TV.
