13. QUIZ2² + 3n = 16note: pocong wibu ;-;​


SPLSV —

[tex] \sf {2}^{2} + 3n = 16[/tex]

[tex] \sf(2 \times 2) + 3n = 16[/tex]

[tex] \sf4 + 3n = 16[/tex]

[tex] \sf3n = 16 - 4[/tex]

[tex] \sf3n = 12[/tex]

[tex] \sf n = \frac{12}{3} [/tex]

[tex] \sf n = 4[/tex]

» Sistem Persamaan Linear Satu Variabel «

_______________________________________

[tex] {2}^{2} + 3n = 16 \\ 3n + {2}^{2} = 16 \\ 3n = 16 - {2}^{2} \\ 3n = 16 - (2 \times 2) \\ 3n = 16 - 4 \\ 3n = 12 \\ n = 12 \div 3 \\ \boxed{ \purple{n = 4}}[/tex]
