S dalam kurung maksudnya Subjek. Ganti sesuai petunjuk berikut
Kalau subjeknya :
1.) Tunggal
Saya = me, myself
Dia (pr) = her, herself
Dia (lk) = him, himself
2.) Jamak
Kami = us, ourselves
Mereka = them, themselves
