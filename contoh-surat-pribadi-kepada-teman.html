1. contoh surat pribadi terhadap teman


Bandar Lampung, 1 April 2015

Untuk Sahabatku Dina
di Bogor

Assalamualaikum. . .

Hay Dina, bagaimana kabarmu di sana? Aku di sini baik-baik saja. Aku berharap kamu dan keluarga di sana juga selalu dalam keadaan sehat. Sudah lama ya kita tidak bertemu? Kalau tidak salah terakhir kita bertemu ketika kita lulus dari SMP dan sekarang kita sudah mau lulus SMA. Pasti kali ini kau sudah bertambah tinggi atau bahkan sudah lebiih tinggi daripadaku. Eh, bagaimana keadaanmu di Bogor? apakah benar yang sering dikatakan bahwa Bogor adalah kota hujan? 

Dina, sebenarnya aku menulis surat ini karena akau sangat kangen kepadamu. Aku rindu masa-masa kita bersama, ketika saling menghabiskan waktu dan bercanda, Kapan ya masa-masa itu akan terulang kembali? Dina aku juga ingin mengabarkan kepadamu bahwa setelah lulus dari SMA ini aku akan melanjutkan pendidikanku di Universitas Indonesia. Aku telah lama ingin kuliah di salah satu universitas terbaik di negeri ini. Tolong bantu aku doa ya? Lalu bagaimana denganmu ? Mudah-mudahan kita bisa belajar bersama lagi di sana.

Sudah dulu ya, sampaikan salamku kepada ayah, ibu dan Ara di sana. Aku tunggu kabar darimu dan semoga kita bisa segera bertemu.

Wassalamualaikum . . 

Salam Manis,
Sahabatmu

4. Contoh surat pribadi buat teman yang diseberang


This is an example of the letter

To my long distance best friend, 

We have been inseparable for so long that now that you’re far away, I feel like a part of me is missing without you. For years, we spent night after night sharing a bed, and laughing about stupid people we didn’t like, and complaining about parents. We grew up together, really. We were both babies when we first met, only second graders, but even then we knew that our new found friendship was something more than playing on the playground and G-rated sleepovers. I found not only my best friend in you, but my person, my soul mate, and the twin sister I never knew I wanted, but found impossible to let go of.

Things haven’t always been easy. We have had our fair share of fights. Our fair share of disagreements. Our fair share of just about everything. But what is different about us is the way we have always gotten through things. You have always been a rock in my life, even when everything around you could be crumbling down. I know now that you’re only a phone call away, but that doesn’t constitute for our long, long late night talks about our worries for the future, and where we wanted to go in life. It’s funny because we talked forever about our futures being so similar. We talked about leaving home as soon as possible, starting a career, finding husbands (that could handle both of us), and raising families down the street from each other. So much has changed since we thought those dreams were possible. We both left, you already with a boyfriend, me entirely too single, and began foraging our ways. Now I’m back in our home state with a boyfriend, you’re still away with a boyfriend, and we both have separate dreams, to live in separate states, with separate zip codes, and separate children, with two completely separate lives. I never thought I would lose you, but as I’m sitting here writing this, I’m realizing that being a best friend is more than living down the street and having a kid with your middle name. It is about loving the person unconditionally and loving who they love and who they have become, more than loving your relationship.

So in this letter to you, I want you to know that wherever you may go, and whatever you may do, I have always been here and I will continue to support you in any and all endeavors. We may not go to parties and make stupid decisions like we once did, but there will always be a time for us to crack a bottle of wine and complain about things that don’t matter. We may not share a bed on countless sleepovers, but we will always be welcome in one another’s home any hour, day or night, no matter the cost. And most importantly, we may not be each others first loves anymore (because lets be real, you were basically my emotional live in boyfriend), but we will find love in seeing one another happy, and seeing one another’s lives unfold to what we always talked about.

To my long distance best friend, things haven’t always gone our way, and although we have gone our separate ways, keep each other close, our families closer, and remember that if you ever need to come home, Mom and I will be ready to greet you. Love you B.
