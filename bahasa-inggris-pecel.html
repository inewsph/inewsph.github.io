Nasi pecel is a Javanese rice dish served with pecel (cooked vegetables and peanut sauce).The vegetables are usually kangkung or water spinach, long beans, cassava leaves, papaya leaves, and in East Java often used kembang turi. It tastes best when eaten with fried tempeh and traditional cracker called peyek. It is popular in East and Central Java.

19. buatkan cara membuat nasi pecel dalam bahasa inggris!!!


HOW TO MAKE NASI PECEL

You will need : 
A mixing bowl and spoon 
Freshly boiled water
4 to 5 persons who enjoy fine food

Ingredients :
100gms. packet of Pecel Hippy Paste
Plain white boiled rice
Fresh water spinach (or any of your favourite fresh green leaf vegetable)
Fresh green beans
Fresh bean sprouts

Steps :
Step #1
Put the Pecel Hippy paste in the bowl.

Step #2
Add Hot Water to the paste.(the less water you add, the thicker the consistencyand the spicier the taste).

Step #3Using the back of your spoonmix the Pecel Hippy paste with the hot wateruntil the sauce is smooth and slightly runny.

Step #4Once smooth, pour the Pecel Hippy sauce overthe white boiled rice, water spinach,green beans and bean sprouts.
And for that truly Javanese experienceserve your Nasi Pecel withfried Tempe and Peyek.
