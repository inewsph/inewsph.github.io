3. 10 fontoh kalimat agreement dan disagreement


Berikut 10 contoh kalimat agreement:

You are right!I agree.That’s right.Sounds great!Exactly!Couldn’t agree more.Great idea.I guess so.I think so.Absolutely agree.I feel the same.

Sedangkan 10 contoh kalimat disagreement:

I disagree.I don’t think so.I’m not sure about that.That’s unacceptable!That’s not a good idea.I can not go along with that.Absolutely no.I wouldn’t say that.That’s unacceptable!I don’t feel the same.Pembahasan

Ketika berbincang sehari-hari, atau ketika dihadapkan dengan orang lain, sering kali kita dituntut untuk memberi persetujuan atau menolak persetujuan atas sesuatu hal. Memberi persetujuan dinyatakan lewat kalimat agreement sementara menolak persetujuan dinyatakan lewat kalimat disagreement. Kalimat agreement dilontarkan ketika kita sejalan atau setuju dengan pernyataan atau ide atau pendapat seseorang. Cara mengekspresikannya lewat contoh kalimat berikut ini:

You are right! (kamu benar).I agree (aku setuju).That’s right (itu benar).Sounds great (kedengarannya bagus).Exactly! (tepat sekali!)Couldn’t agree more (sangat setuju).Great idea (ide bagus).I guess so (aku rasa begitu).I think so (aku pikir begitu).Absolutely agree (sangat setuju).I feel the same (aku merasakan yang sama).

Kalimat disagreement disampaikan ketika kita tidak sejalan, tidak setuju atau kurang sependapat dengan pernyataan, ide, gagasan atau pendapat seseorang. Contoh kalimatnya ada banyak, antara lain:

I disagree (aku tidak setuju).I don’t think so (aku tak berpikir begitu).I’m not sure about that (aku tak yakin dengan hal tersebut).That’s unacceptable! (itu tak bisa diterima!)That’s not a good idea (itu bukan ide bagus).I can not go along with that (aku tak sejalan dengan hal itu).Absolutely no (tentu saja tidak).I wouldn’t say that (aku tak akan berkata demikian).That’s unacceptable (itu tak bisa diterima).I don’t feel the same (aku tak merasakan yang sama).I doubt that (aku meragukan itu).Totally disagree (sangat tak setuju).Pelajari Lebih LanjutMateri tentang affirmative agreement and negative agreement https://brainly.co.id/tugas/13737639Materi tentang 10 kalimat ekspresi agreement disagreement dalam bahasa Inggris https://brainly.co.id/tugas/12610317Materi tentang give your opinion whether you agree / somewhat agree / disagree on a matter https://brainly.co.id/tugas/16172315

Jawaban:

Kalimat agreement (percaya):

1. Siti: "I agree with Udin. You should go to the doctor, Lina".

2. Beni: "I agree with Dayu. Udin please go back to work..."

3. Edo: "You're right, Siti. We are too serious..."

4. Lina: "I agree with Siti and Edo. Come on everybody. Please stand up..."

5. Udin: "That's a smart idea. everybody let's stop working..."

6. Dayu: "I think the best to do is go home, Lina".

7. Siti: "Udin, to feel better, drink a big glass of water and have a little exercise here".

Kalimat disagreement (tidak percaya):

1. Beni: "I don't think that's a good idea. Just take a rest and drink a lot of water, Lina".

2. Edo: "I don't agree with you, Siti. Too much medicine is not good".

3. Dayu: "I don't think that's a good idea. Let's finish our text..."
