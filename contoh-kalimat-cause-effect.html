2. contoh kalimat effect + connector + cause​


Jawaban:

Dalam bahasa Inggris, arti dari kalimat cause and effect adalah sebab akibat. Yaitu, jenis kalimat majemuk yang memiliki sebab dan dapat menimbulkan akibat. Cause berarti sesuatu yang terjadi, sedangkan effect merupakan konsekuensi atau akibat dari adanya kejadian tersebut.

Biasanya, kalimat ini bercirikan kata atau frasa penanda yang disebut konektor. Karena sering digunakan bersamaan dengan kalimat degree of comparison, bisa sembari belajar keduanya! Apa aja ya konektor dalam cause and effect? Pahami penjelasan berikut yuk. Ada rumus serta contoh kalimatnya juga lho!

Cause and effect terbagi menjadi dua, yaitu yang diikuti oleh noun, verb atau klausa. Untuk bisa menguasai, kamu perlu membandingkan dulu antara kalimat cause dan juga effect.

Konektor: due to, owing to, because of, as a result of, as a consequence of

8. contoh kalimat cause and effect conjunction


Conjuntion adalah kata hubung yang menghubungkan kata, phrase, kalimat/klausa menjadi satu kalimat. Conjunction yang menyatakan sebab akibat (cause and effect) diantaranya: because, since, as,  because of, so, therefore, inspite of, despite of, dll.

Pembahasan

Beberapa Contoh kalimat yang menggunakan conjunction cause and effect:

1. Since you don't believe me, I won't say a word anymore.  

2. It is blackout, so I light some candles inside my house.  

3. The departure of eagle air has been delayed due to the bad weather.  

4. My nephew cried so loudly because of your dog.  

5. Kiki broke his mother's vase. consequently, he must buy a new vase.

6. She got flue: therefore she met a doctor.

7. On an account of the delicious taste, l ate five pieces of this red velvet.

8. as you got the first rank last semester, father gave you some money.

9. Randy is so angry that he doesn't want to talk with me.  

10. Stella feels so sleepy because she is so tired this day.  

11. Rania has headache. therefore, she feels so dizzy.  

12. People in the village work together to build a bridge since they still

     have    the sense of mutual coorperation (gotong royong).  

13. The students have to memorize the struggle of indonesian heroes.

    Thus, they can do their best for the Indonesia like them.  

14. The man helped the policeman to catch a thief, so that the thief

    can be   arrested and bring to police office.  

15. l got any stressful due to my deadline job.

CONJUNCTION  

Conjunction adalah kata hubung yang digunakan untuk menggabungkan suatu kata, phrase, klausa atau kalimat menjadi sebuah kalimat. Dalam menggunakan kata hubung dalam sebuah kalimat yang menyatakan clause and effect dapat menggunakan beberapa kata berikut ini:  

1. So (Terlalu)  

Contoh:  

She ate too much this morning, so she fell asleep in the afternoon. (Ia terlalu banyak makan pagi ini, jadi ia tertidur siangnya.)  

2. As (Karena)  

Contoh:  

She deserved to get it, as she worked hard for it . (Ia layak mendapatkannya, karena dia telah bekarja keras untuk itu.)  

3. Since (Karena)  

Contoh: Since his father told him to study hard for his future so, he studies abroad now. (Semenjak ayahnya berkata kepadanya untuk belajar dengan giat , Ia bersekolah di luar negeri sekarang.)  

4. Because (Karena)  

Contoh:  

They go to hospital because my uncle gets in an accident. (Mereka pergi kerumah sakit karena pamanku kecelakaan.)  

5. Due to (Karena)  

Contoh:  

The young man was lost his foot, due to car accident. (Lelaki muda itu kehilangan kakinya, karena kecelakaan mobil)  

6. Hence (Karenanya, karena itulah, oleh karenanya)  

Contoh:  

He wants to take a leak; hence, he looks so hurry. (Ia ingin buang air kecil; karena itulah, ia terlihat amat terburu-buru.)  

7. Consequently (sebagai akibatnya/hasilnya)  

Contoh:  

He was a street racer back in that day; consequently, his broken legs. (Ia seorang pembalap jalanan kala itu; konsekuensinya, kakinya yang patah.)  

8. Therefore (Oleh karenanya)  

Contoh:  

1. She spent hours for just writing that article. Therefore, it could be finished before the deadline. (Ia menghabiskan waktu berjam-jam untuk menulis artikel itu. Karena nya, itu bisa diselesaikan sebelum tenggatnya.)  

2. They never argued about my decisions. Therefore, we always ended up as a great team. (Mereka tidak pernah mengeluh tentang keputusan-keputusanku. Karena itulah, kita selalu berakhir sebagai team yang baik.)  

9. As a result (Hasilnya, sehingga)  

Contoh:

He always saves all his earnings. As a result, his big luxury uphill mansion. (Ia selalu menabung semua pemasukannya. Sehingga, Puri besar dan megah diatas tebing miliknya.)  

10. For this reason (Karena alasan itulah)

Contoh:

1. He can play that game in days without doing anything else. For this reason, I call him ‘maniac’. (Ia mampu memainkan permainan ini berhari-hari tanpa melakukan apapun. Karena alasan inilah, aku menyebut dia ‘maniak’.)

2. My aunt always hugs me so hard. For this reason, I always hide when she comes to my place. (Bibiku selalu memelukku dengan erat. Karena alasan inilah, aku selalu bersembunyi ketika ia datang ketempatku.)

25. 2 contoh kalimat cause and effect tentang membuang sampah


Kalimat cause and effect tentang membuang sampah adalah sebagai berikut:

Various cancers are possibly caused by throwing garbage which contains toxic chemicals and metals.

Because of throwing garbage anywhere, land pollution level is increasing every year.

Pembahasan

Untuk mengungkapkan cause and effect dalam bahasa Inggris, kita menggunakan signal words berikut:

due tobecause ofowing tothanks toas a consequence ofas a result ofbecausesinceasfor

Nah, untuk menunjukan cause and effect dalam kalimat ada dua jenis yakni yang diikuti kata benda (followed by noun) dan yang diikuti kata kerja (followed by verb).

Followed by noun

Signal word/phrase + Cause + Effect

Effect + Signal word/phrase + Cause

Due to Jen’s laziness, she didn’t pass the Math test.

Signal phrase: Due toCause: Jen’s lazinessEffect: She didn’t pass the test

He was sick as a consequence of his ignorance.

Signal phrase: As a consequenceCause: His ignoranceEffect: He was sick

Followed by verb

Signal word/phrase + Cause + Effect

Effect + Signal word/phrase + Cause

Because Jen was lazy, she didn’t pass the Math test.

Signal phrase: BecauseCause: Jen was lazyEffect: She didn’t pass the Math test

As he ignored his health, he will be sick.

Signal word: AsCause: He ignoredEffect: He will be sick

Text 1

2. Membuat kalimat Cause and Effect sentences dari kalimat tersebut

His town is the cleanest town in the province so it got the Adipura Award. The people did not care so the town was dirty and messy. The government and the people have been working hard together so it is clean and beautiful now. There were not many trees so it was hot and dry. Three years ago the mayor launched a program to clean the town and to plant trees so it is clean and green now. The government planned the program together with them so the people in town are happy to take part in it. He often has bad coughs and gets sick easily so he believes 100% that smoking is not good for his health. He knows it is not easy to cut down cigarettes so he keeps trying.Vina is the eldest of four children in her family so she does the chores and takes care of her younger sisters and brother all the time.She can manage her time very well so she can always finish her homework, and she always gets good marks.  Pembahasan

Bagian kalimat yang dicetak tebal adalah Cause atau sebab dan bagian kalimat yang dicetak miring adalah Effect atau akibat. Kata penghubung yang digunakan adalah so yang berarti sehingga. Hal ini menyatakan, bagian kalimat setelah kata hubung so merupakan akibat dari kegiatan atau aktivitas atau hal yang diungkapkan sebelumnya. Contohnya pada kalimat pertama:

His town is the cleanest town in the province so it got the Adipura Award.

Artinya: Kota tempat tinggalnya adalah kota paling bersih di provinsi itu sehingga kota tersebut memperoleh penghargaan Adipura.

Kalimat di atas merupakan contoh complex sentence. It consists of 1 dependent clause and 1 independent clause.
