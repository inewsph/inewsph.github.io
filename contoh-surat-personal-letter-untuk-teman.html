16. contoh personal letter dan balasan nya untuk teman sebangku​


Jawaban:

Personal Letter

Dear friend,

How are you doing? I hope this letter finds you in good health. I am writing to catch up with you and see how things have been going for you. It has been such a long time since we last talked and I have been missing our conversations.

I have been busy lately with school and my part-time job, but I always find time to think about you. I would love to hear about your life and all the things that have been keeping you busy. I am also eager to hear about your future plans and what you want to do after graduation.

I hope you will take some time to write back and let me know how you are doing. I miss our friendship and I hope that we can reconnect soon.

Take care and I hope to hear from you soon.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]

Reply

Dear [Your Name],

I am so glad to receive your letter and to hear from you again. I have been doing well and keeping busy with my studies and extracurricular activities. Thank you for asking!

It has been a while since we last talked, but I have been thinking about you too. I have been busy with my own life, but I would love to catch up with you and hear about all the things that have been going on in your life. I am so excited to hear about your future plans and what you want to do after graduation.

I hope that we can make plans soon to catch up and spend some time together. I have missed our friendship and I would love to strengthen our bond once again.

Take care and I look forward to hearing from you soon.

Best regards,

[Your Friend's Name]

21. cara membuat surat personal lette dan berikan contoh personal letter termudah​


Jawaban:

langkah – langkah menulis berbagai contoh personal letter, mengetahui

-Tema personal letter,

-Kaidah kebahasaan,

-Penerima personal letter,

-Susunan personal letter

Saat ingin membuat personal letter, kita haru tau struktur penulisan Personal Letter, yaitu:

-Date (Tanggal)

-Adress (Alamat)

-Salutation and Name (Salam dan Nama)

-Introduction (Pembukaan)

-Body (Isi Surat)

-Closing (Penutup)

-Greetings for Closing (Salam Penutup)

-Signature (Tanda Tangan)

Contoh:

Jakarta, 18 November 2021

Dear Arsi,

How are you? I hope that you are okay. I miss you so much. And I promise that I will visit you during the school vacation. Let’s make a plan for holiday together. How about your school? I hope that everything is okay and we can graduate together. See you soon Arsi.

Sincerely,

Kino
