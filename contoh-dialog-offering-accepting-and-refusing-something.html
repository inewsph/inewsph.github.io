9. Contoh dialog asking and offering something


Contoh dialog asking and offering something!

==================

BRIEF CORRECTION

¶ Asking something ( ungkapan meminta suatu pertolongan terhadap orang lain )

Contoh berdasarkan dialog :

¶ Would you mind taking the broom in the kitchen ? ( Apakah kamu keberatan ambil sapu di dapur )

¶ If you don't mind, Could you sweep my room's floor !( Jika tidak keberatan, bisakah kamu menyapu lantai kamarku )

¶ Offering something ( ungkapan menawarkan suatu bantuan ( pertolongan ) terhadap orang lain yang memerlukan .

Contoh berdasarkan dialog :

¶ Do you need any help ? ( Apakah kamu perlu bantuan )

¶ What can I do more for you ?( Apa yang bisa aku lakukan lagi untuk mu )

°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°°

Dialog of asking and Offering something!

( In Dee's house )

Roy. : Good afternoon, Dee?

Dee.: Good afternoon, Roy ?

Roy. : How are you ? What are you doing ?

Dee. : I'm fine. I'm cleaning my room.

Roy. : DO YOU NEED ANY HELP?

Dee. : With pleasure. WOULD YOU MIND TAKING THE BROOM IN THE KITCHEN , Roy ?

Roy. : Of course , wait a minute. ( 3 seconds later ) Here is the broom?

WHAT CAN I DO MORE FOR YOU , Dee ?

Dee. : IF YOU DON'T MIND, COULD YOU SWEEP MY ROOM'S FLOOR ?

Roy. : Never minds, Dee.

Dee. : Thank you very much for your help, Roy!

================

15. contoh dialog asking giving accepting and refusing help yang singkat?


kelas : VIII
pelajaran : Bahasa Inggris
kategori : expressions
kata kunci : asking, refusing, accepting, help

pembahasan
dialog asking giving accepting and refusing help
you : Can you help me, please? (asking for help)
your friend : Sure. what can I do for you? (accepting for help)
you : My bicycle was broken. Can you repair it? (asking for help)
your friend : I am sorry. I can't repair your bicycle. I don't have the tools to repair it. (refusing for help)
you : It's okay. I will ask my father to repair it.
your friend : that'll be a good idea.

dialog 2
you : Mother, would you help me? (asking for help)
your mother : Sure. I would be glad to help you (accepting for help)
you : I can't do my homework. Could you explain it for me, Mom? (asking for help)
your mother : I wish I could help you but I don't understand this subject, my dear. I'm sorry. (refusing for help)
you : It's okay, Mom. I'll find it again myself.
your mother : What can I do for you, dear? (offering help)
you : Thanks, Mom, but I can manage myself. (refusing help)
your mother : Okay. Be patient, my dear.
