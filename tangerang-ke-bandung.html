20. Kuis [+50]: Tentukan banyaknya susunan kata dari BANDUNG OVER TANGERANG SORRY BEIBEH


Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

-BANDUNG

unsur ganda = N (2)

7!/2!

= 7×6×5×4×3×2×1 / 2×1

= 5040 / 2

= 2.520 susunan

-OVER

total unsur = 4 huruf

unsur ganda = -

4! = 4×3×2×1 = 24 susunan

-TANGERANG

total unsur = 9 huruf

unsur ganda = A (2) N (2) G (2)

9!/2!.2!.2!

= 9×8×7×6×5×4×3×2 / 2×2×2

= 362880 / 8

= 45.360 susunan

-SORRY

total unsur = 5 huruf

unsur ganda = R (2)

5!/2!

= 5×4×3×2×1 / 2×1

= 120/2

= 60 susunan

-BEIBEH

total unsur = 6 huruf

unsur ganda = B (2) E (2)

6!/2!.2!

= 6×5×4×3×2 / 2×2

= 720 / 4

= 180 susunan
