21. buat contoh 10 past countinouse tensemenggunakan when/while rumus S+TOBE+WAS/WERE+INGmenggunakan when/while


Rumus SIMPLE PAST CONTINUOUS :
(+) S + was/were + Ving + O
(-) S + was/were + not + Ving + O
(?) Was/were + S + Ving + O +?

SIMPLE PAST CONTINUOUS digunakan pada kalimat yang sudah terjadi di masa lampau, dimana pada saat tertentu sedang dikerjakan.

SIMPLE PAST TENSE vs SIMPLE PAST CONTINUOUS

Gunakan SIMPLE PAST CONTINUOUS pada kalimat kondisi yang dilakukan terlebih dahulu.

Dan gunakan SIMPLE PAST TENSE pada kalimat kondisi yang dilakukan setelah kalimat kondisi yang pertama. Biasanya 2 kalimat tersebut dihubungkan dengan kata WHEN

Jika 2 kalimat kondisi dilakukan bersamaan, gunakan SIMPLE PAST CONTINUOUS pada kedua kalimat tersebut. Biasanya menggunakan WHILE untuk menghubungkan 2 kalimat tersebut.

Contoh kalimat :
1. The children were watching TV when their mother called

2. When the teacher was teaching, the headmaster came in

3. Lisa was doing her homework, when her brother disturbed her

4. My mother was cooking dinner, when my father came home

5. We were reading book at the library, when the bell rang

6. She was cooking, while he was cleaning the dishes

7. I was painting, while my sister was drawing

8. They were going to school, while their father was going to the office

9. My mother was gardening, while I was watering the flowers

10. Mrs. Smith was feeding the baby while her children were playing football

25. fungsinya when or while + contohnya


When :
Menandakan suatu kegiatan yang diganggu oleh kegiatan lain.

Contoh : I was reading when he called.
Arti : Aku sedang membaca saat dia memanggil.

While :
Menandakan 2 kegiatan yang berjalan bersamaan (simultaneously).

Contoh : I was watching TV while he cooked dinner.
Arti : Aku sedang menonton TV selagi dia masak makan malam.
