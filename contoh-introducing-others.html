4. Contoh introducing others formal dan informal


Jawaban:

-Formal Introductions

John: Excuse me, are you Dr. James?

Dr. James: Yes I am. Can I help you?

John: May I introduce ourself? I am John and this is my friend Jim. We are from a book fair organizer.

Dr. James: Hello John & Jim. How do you do.

Jim: How do you do. We are here to offer you a prime stand for your publications at our book fair.

Dr. James: It’s nice to hear an offer from you. But I am afraid I don’t have much time right now. Can you give the proposal to my secretary?

John: OK. I will give the proposal to your secretary.

Dr. James: Great. Nice to see you.

Jim: Goodbye. Pleased to meet you.

-Informal Introductions

John: What’s up bro!

Jack: Pretty good. How are you doing?

John: Fine. Who is with you?

Jack: Oh. Meet my cousin Jimmy, he came here to vacation.

John: Hello Jimmy, nice to meet you. I am John.

Jimmy: Hello John, glad to see you. Call me Jim.

John: Got to go now.

Jack: OK. See you!

John+Jimmy: See you!

Penjelasan:

kalo FORMAL itu biasanya dipake kalo bicara sama oarang yg lebih tua

kalo INFORMAL itu lebih ke temen sepantaran

18. Contoh soal bahasa inggris 10 soal tentang introducing self and others


Jawaban :
--Question about Introducing Self--
1) Hello, guys! Let me .... myself. My name is Hanna. I come from Bekasi. Nice to meet you.
The correct word to fill the blanks is ...
==> introduce

2) The correct expression to introducing self is ...
==> Let me introduce myself

3) Hello, please meet my cousin. ... name is Ghani.
The correct pronoun is ...
==> His

4) Chaca-cousin-is-she-named-my
The correct arrangement is ...
==> She is my cousin named Chaca

5) Things that usually used in a introducing text are ..
==> name, birth date, age, class, address, hobby, etc

6) The expression that usually uses in the end of the introucing text is ...
==> Nice to meet you / Let's be friends // etc

7) The purpose of introducing ourselves is ...
==> to let people knows about us

8) ... My name is Zahra.
The correct greetings is ...
==> Hello / Hi / Good morning

9) My name is Alfi. I just ... here two days ago. Let's be friends
The correct verb is ...
==> moved

10) Through introducing self, we can ... friends and ... more people
The correct words is
==> make, know
