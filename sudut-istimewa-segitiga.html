4. Hitunglah perbandingan sisi-sisi segitiga siku-siku dengan sudut istimewa di bawah ini.


Jawab:

segitiga sikusiku

sudut istimewa , 30 ,60 , 45, 90

perbandingan sisi

1. Δ ABC  , < A = 90°, <B = 30°, <C =  60°

maka  perbandingan sisi sisi sudut 30, 60 , 90

AC : AB : BC = 1 : √3 : 2

BC =  6 cm, maka

AC = 1/2 x 6  cm =  3 cm

BC =  √3 / 2 x  6 cm = 3√3 cm

3. Δ DEF, < E = 90 , <D = 45, <F =45

perbandingan sisi sisi  sudut 45, 45 , 90

DE: EF : DF  = 1 : 1 : √2

panjang EF = 2  cm, maka :

DE = 1/1  x 2 cm = 2 cm

DF = √2/1  x 2  cm = 2√2 cm

PEMBAHASAN

Sudut istimewa adalah sudut tertentu yang bila disubstitusikan ke perbandingan trigonometri seperti sinus, cosinus, tangen, cosecan, secan, dan cotangen nilainya mudah ditentukan tanpa menggunakan kalkulator. Adapun yang termasuk sudut istimewa antara lain:

[tex]\boxed{\begin{array}{c}\underline{\sf Kuadran\:I}\\\sf0^o\\\sf30^o\\\sf45^o\\\sf60^o\\\sf90^o\end{array}}\boxed{\begin{array}{c}\underline{\sf Kuadran\:II}\\\sf120^o\\\sf135^o\\\sf150^o\\\sf180^o\\\sf\end{array}}\boxed{\begin{array}{c}\underline{\sf Kuadran\:III}\\\sf210^o\\\sf225^o\\\sf240^o\\\sf270^o\\\sf\end{array}}\boxed{\begin{array}{c}\underline{\sf Kuadran\:IV}\\\sf300^o\\\sf315^o\\\sf330^o\\\sf360^o\\\sf\end{array}}[/tex]

ㅤ

Pada perbandingan sinus, cosinus, dan tangen nilai sudut istimewanya antara lain:

[tex]\boxed{\begin{array}{c|c|c|c}\sf\underline{~~~\alpha~~~}&\underline{~~~\sf sin\:\alpha~~~}&\underline{~~~\sf cos\:\alpha~~~}&\underline{~~~\sf tan\:\alpha~~~}\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf0^o&\sf0&\sf1&\sf0\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf30^o&\sf\dfrac{1}{2}&\sf\dfrac{1}{2}\sqrt{3}&\sf\dfrac{1}{3}\sqrt{3}\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf45^o&\sf\dfrac{1}{2}\sqrt{2}&\sf\dfrac{1}{2}\sqrt{2}&\sf1\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf60^o&\sf\dfrac{1}{2}\sqrt{3}&\sf\dfrac{1}{2}&\sf\sqrt{3}\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf90^o&\sf1&\sf0&\sf-\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf120^o&\sf\dfrac{1}{2}\sqrt{3}&\sf -\dfrac{1}{2}&\sf-\sqrt{3}\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf135^o&\sf\dfrac{1}{2}\sqrt{2}&\sf-\dfrac{1}{2}\sqrt{2}&\sf-1\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf150^o&\sf\dfrac{1}{2}&\sf-\dfrac{1}{2}\sqrt{3}&\sf-\dfrac{1}{3}\sqrt{3}\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf180^o&\sf0&\sf-1&\sf0\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf210^o&\sf-\dfrac{1}{2}&\sf-\dfrac{1}{2}\sqrt{3}&\sf\dfrac{1}{3}\sqrt{3}\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf225^o&\sf-\dfrac{1}{2}\sqrt{2}&\sf-\dfrac{1}{2}\sqrt{2}&\sf1\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf240^o&\sf-\dfrac{1}{2}\sqrt{3}&\sf-\dfrac{1}{2}&\sf\sqrt{3}\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf270^o&\sf-1&\sf0&\sf-\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf300^o&\sf-\dfrac{1}{2}\sqrt{3}&\sf\dfrac{1}{2}&\sf-\sqrt{3}\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf315^o&\sf-\dfrac{1}{2}\sqrt{2}&\sf\dfrac{1}{2}\sqrt{2}&\sf-1\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf330^o&\sf\dfrac{1}{2}&\sf-\dfrac{1}{2}\sqrt{3}&\sf-\dfrac{1}{3}\sqrt{3}\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf360^o&\sf0&\sf1&\sf0\\\sf&\tt&\tt&\tt\end{array}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{\begin{array}{c|c|c|c}\sf\underline{~~~\alpha~~~}&\underline{~~~\sf csc\:\alpha~~~}&\underline{~~~\sf sec\:\alpha~~~}&\underline{~~~\sf cot\:\alpha~~~}\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf0^o&\sf-&\sf1&\sf-\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf30^o&\sf2&\sf\dfrac{2}{3}\sqrt{3}&\sf\sqrt{3}\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf45^o&\sf\sqrt{2}&\sf\sqrt{2}&\sf1\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf60^o&\sf\dfrac{2}{3}\sqrt{3}&\sf2&\sf\dfrac{1}{3}\sqrt{3}\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf90^o&\sf1&\sf-&\sf0\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf120^o&\sf\dfrac{2}{3}\sqrt{3}&\sf -2&\sf-\dfrac{1}{3}\sqrt{3}\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf135^o&\sf\sqrt{2}&\sf-\sqrt{2}&\sf-1\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf150^o&\sf2&\sf-\dfrac{2}{3}\sqrt{3}&\sf-\sqrt{3}\\&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf180^o&\sf-&\sf-1&\sf-\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf210^o&\sf-2&\sf-\dfrac{2}{3}\sqrt{3}&\sf\sqrt{3}\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf225^o&\sf-\sqrt{2}&\sf-\sqrt{2}&\sf1\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf240^o&\sf-\dfrac{2}{3}\sqrt{3}&\sf-2&\sf\dfrac{1}{3}\sqrt{3}\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf270^o&\sf-1&\sf0&\sf-\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf300^o&\sf-\dfrac{2}{3}\sqrt{3}&\sf2&\sf-\dfrac{1}{3}\sqrt{3}\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf315^o&\sf-\sqrt{2}&\sf\sqrt{2}&\sf-1\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf330^o&\sf-2&\sf\dfrac{2}{3}\sqrt{3}&\sf-\sqrt{3}\\\tt&\tt&\tt&\tt\\\sf360^o&\sf-&\sf1&\sf-\\\sf&\tt&\tt&\tt\end{array}}[/tex]

Jawab:

[tex]\begin{aligned}\sf\dfrac{sin~30^o+tan~45^o-csc~60^o}{sec~30^o+cos~60^o+cot~45^o}&=\sf\dfrac{\dfrac{1}{2}+1-\dfrac{2}{3}\sqrt{3}}{\dfrac{2}{3}\sqrt{3}+\dfrac{1}{2}+1}\times\dfrac{6}{6}\\&=\sf\dfrac{3+6-4\sqrt{3}}{4\sqrt{3}+3+6}\\&=\sf\dfrac{9-4\sqrt{3}}{4\sqrt{3}+9}\\&=\sf\dfrac{9-4\sqrt{3}}{9+4\sqrt{3}}\times\dfrac{9-4\sqrt{3}}{9-4\sqrt{3}}\\&=\sf\dfrac{81-2\cdot9\cdot4\sqrt{3}+16\cdot3}{81-16\cdot3}\\&=\sf\dfrac{81-72\sqrt{3}+48}{81-48}\\&=\sf\dfrac{129-72\sqrt{3}}{33}\\&=\sf\dfrac{3(43-24\sqrt{3})}{33}\\&=\sf\dfrac{43-24\sqrt{3}}{11}\end{aligned}[/tex]

→ salah satu sudutnya 90°

→ 180° - 32° - 100° = 47°

= 90° - 63° = 27°
