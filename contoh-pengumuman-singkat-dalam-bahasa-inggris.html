  • 10. 1. Definisi dan fungsi Descriptive text. Descriptive text adalah suatu jenis teks yang bertujuan untuk menjelaskan atau mendeskripsikan orang, binatang, tempat ataupun suatu benda. Umumnya, yang dideskripsikan adalah bentuk, ciri, ataupun sifatnya.2. Struktur teks (generic structure).Adapun struktur dari descriptive text adalah sebagai berikut:• Identification: pengenalan objek atau hal yang akan dideskripsikan.• Description: penginformasian ciri-ciri objek, misalnya sifat-sifat psikologis perilaku, tampilan fisik, dan yang lainnya secara spesifik.3. Unsur kebahasaan.Descriptive text biasanya menggunakan unsur kebahasaan sebagai berikut:• Noun: kata benda yang spesifik, misalnya my best friend, my dog, Borobudur Temple, dan lain – lain. Selain itu, terkadang juga menggunakan kata benda yang mendapat pemberian kata sifat, misalnya an intelligent student dan a big garden.• Simple present tense: menggunakan kata kerja bentuk pertama (verb1) dan juga kata kerja yang dapat menggambarkan keadaan objek atau mengisyaratkan kepemilikan, misalnya my house has 2 living rooms, Sinta is beautiful, dan lain – lain.• Adjective: kata sifat digunakan untuk menjelaskan objek. • Figurative language: menggunakan bahasa figuratif seperti simile atau metafora sebagai cara untuk memberikan ilustrasi perbandingan, misalnya: My throat is as dry as a dessert (tenggorokanku sekering gurun).Itulah penjelasan mengenai definisi, fungsi, struktur teks, dan juga unsur kebahasaan dari descriptive text. Subject: Importance of Environmental Awareness

Ladies and Gentlemen,

Good morning to all of you. I stand here today to talk about a topic that affects us all, and that is the importance of environmental awareness.

As we all know, the world we live in today is facing numerous environmental challenges, such as climate change, deforestation, and pollution. These issues not only affect our health and well-being, but also the future of our planet.

However, it is not too late to act. Each and every one of us has the power to make a positive impact on the environment. By being environmentally aware and making small changes in our daily lives, such as reducing, reusing, and recycling, we can help to protect our planet for future generations.

Let us all make a pledge to become more environmentally aware and to take action to protect the environment. We have the power to make a difference, so let us not waste this opportunity.

Thank you for your time and attention.

Indonesia:

Judul: Pentingnya Kesadaran Lingkungan

Selamat pagi untuk kita semua. Saya berdiri di sini hari ini untuk berbicara tentang topik yang mempengaruhi kita semua, yaitu pentingnya kesadaran lingkungan.

Seperti yang kita semua tahu, dunia tempat kita hidup saat ini sedang menghadapi berbagai tantangan lingkungan, seperti perubahan iklim, deforestasi, dan polusi. Masalah ini tidak hanya mempengaruhi kesehatan dan kesejahteraan kita, tetapi juga masa depan planet kita.

Namun, belum terlambat untuk bertindak. Setiap orang memiliki kekuatan untuk membuat dampak positif pada lingkungan. Mari kita semua membuat janji untuk menjadi lebih sadar lingkungan dan bertindak untuk melindungi lingkungan. Kita memiliki kekuatan untuk membuat perbedaan, jadi jangan sia-siakan kesempatan ini.

Terima kasih atas waktu dan perhatian Anda.


5. Contoh pidato umum dalam bahasa inggris dengan tema keindahan dalam bentuk singkat


Save Our Environment

Assalamu’alaikum Warahmatullahi Wabarakatuh

Respected Principal,

Respected Vice Principal,

Respected Teachers,

and My Fellow Friends.

All praise and gratitude is due to Allah SWT, who creates such a beautiful world for us to live in. Peace and blessing be upon the prophet Muhammad SAW, who was sent by Allah as a mercy to all mankind.

It feels privileged that this morning, I am given this opportunity of delivering a speech in front of you all. The topic of the speech I would like to deliver now is “Saving Our Environment”.

I am sure each one of us wants to save environment at any cost. However, saving the environment doesn’t mean to protect only our immediate environment, but also our parks, forest reserves and wildlife sanctuaries too. There is no doubt that we all owe this responsibility to make our Mother Earth; a cleaner and safer place to live in.

The Mother Earth has bequeathed on human beings incredibly beautiful gifts of nature, like we have rivers, mountains, forests and different natural resources that help us to survive on this planet every single day. Therefore, it’s our turn now to work towards in saving the environment from the challenges that are posing a constant threat for the existence of our Mother Earth.

Sadly, there is no part of the earth left that has not been affected by our actions so that our planet’s health – be it in the form of land where we survive and work on, the ozone layer, the water supply, the wildlife or our species – is being put under constant threat. The environment we live in is of course not as conducive as it used to be earlier. There are hordes of chemical industries excreting poisonous chemicals into the rivers and innumerable vehicles releasing dangerous fumes, people disposing waste in a manner which isn’t safe for our environment and ruining it.

Though the problem has aggravated to quite a great extent, but it does have a solution. It’s just that we will have to keep faith in ourselves that we can do something for our planet. So let’s pledge to “Save our Mother Earth” in whatever ways possible for us.

There are various ways which we can do to minimalize the problem. For instance, we can grow more and more trees in our living surroundings as well as where we find an empty piece of land. Or if we have a backyard or a piece of land at our home, we can start planting trees. Plants, as we all know, absorb carbon dioxide for the process of photosynthesis and release oxygen into the environment. It means that a single tree can soak up to one ton of carbon dioxide till the time it lives. No issues, if we don’t have much space, we can even keep small pots of plants in our balcony, doorway or windows.

Secondly, let’s use less of our personal vehicles and habituate ourselves to use public transport or bicycles for short distances. Besides, do not waste energy at home or office. Switch off the device, when not in use. Then we can use fluorescent light bulbs in place of normal bulbs. These bulbs may expensive, but trust me they really help in bringing down our electricity bills because it emits 70 percent less heat in comparison to normal bulbs.

We should also use water wisely; turn off the tap immediately after use. Don’t dump the garbage anywhere and dispose the waste in dustbin. Encourage others too in our family and at our school to adopt these measures and save our environment from the impending doom.
That is all I can say.

Selain itu, terkadang juga menggunakan kata benda yang mendapat pemberian kata sifat, misalnya an intelligent student dan a big garden.• Simple present tense: menggunakan kata kerja bentuk pertama (verb1) dan juga kata kerja yang dapat menggambarkan keadaan objek atau mengisyaratkan kepemilikan, misalnya my house has 2 living rooms, Sinta is beautiful, dan lain – lain.• Adjective: kata sifat digunakan untuk menjelaskan objek. • Figurative language: menggunakan bahasa figuratif seperti simile atau metafora sebagai cara untuk memberikan ilustrasi perbandingan, misalnya: My throat is as dry as a dessert (tenggorokanku sekering gurun).Itulah penjelasan mengenai definisi, fungsi, struktur teks, dan juga unsur kebahasaan dari descriptive text.
