5. Contoh dialog accepting an invitation dan declining invitation


Contoh dialog accepting an invitation dan declining invitation adalah sebagai berikut.


Accepting an invitation

A: Hey, my parents got extra tickets to attend a movie premier.

B: Cool! What movie is that?

A: It’s Bohemian Rhapsody. Have you watched it?

B: Nope, not yet. How about you?

A: Not yet. That’s why I’m planning to ask you come with me to the cinema. Are you free tonight?

B: Sounds great! And yes actually I'm free tonight.

A: Okay, then. I’ll see you today on 7PM sharp.

B: See you!


Declining an invitation

A: Hey, what are you doing on the weekend? Would you like to join me and my brothers for camping?

B: Sounds fun! I’d love to, but I can’t. I have to stay at home, it will be my parent’s 15th year of wedding anniversary. Maybe another time I will be able.

A: Wow, congratulations to them. Anyway, that’s too bad that you can’t join. Hopefully next time you’ll be free.

B: Yes hopefully!

21. 5 expressions of accepting invitation?


5 expressions of accepting invitation:

Sure, why not.Certainly. All right.Of course. I'd be pleased to come.

Pendahuluan

Invitation atau undangan merupakan sebuah surat yang digunakan untuk mengundang seseorang ke tempat acara tersebut.

Undangan di bagi menjadi 2, yaitu:

Formal Invitation

Formal Invitation merupakan sebuah surat undangan yang bersifat resmi.

Informal Invitation

Informal Invitation merupakan sebuah surat undangan yang bersifat tidak resmi. Biasanya digunakan untuk mengundang keluarga dan teman.

Ekspresi mengundang suatu undangan:

1. Invite = Mengundang

Do you want to come to my house? Would you like to come and watch the football game with me?

Ekspresi merespon suatu undangan:

1. Accpet = Menerima

Sure, why not.Certainly.All right.Of course. I'd be pleased to come.

2. Declining = Menolak

Thank you very much, but I'm busy.

Penyelesaian Soal:

5 expressions of accepting invitation?

Jawab:Sure, why not.Certainly.All right. Of course. I'd be pleased to come.

Kesimpulan

Di dalam undangan tersebut terbagi menjadi 2 jenis yaitu formal dan informal. Untuk merespon suatu undangan di bagi menjadi 2, yaitu accepting dan declining.
