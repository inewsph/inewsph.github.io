9. sinopsis film everything everywhere all at once dalam bahasa Inggris 50 kata​


"Everything Everywhere All at Once" is a movie about a scientist who discovers a device that can see all of time at once. He uses it to try to save the future, but realizes that every action he takes in the past changes the future. The film explores the concept of time travel and its consequences.
