7. 1.He doesn't wear a coat. He doesn't wear a jacket.norneither2. They didn't bring umbrellas. They didn't bring rain coats.neithernor3. She doesn't buy a backpack. She doesn't buy a travel bag.neithernor4. The guests don't ride cars. The guests don't ride motorbikes.neithernor5. Mr. Adi doesn't use the escalator. Mr. Adi doesn't use the lift.neithernor​


CORRELATIVE CONJUNCTION

1. He doesn't wear a coat. He doesn't wear a jacket.

➡ He wears neither a coat nor a jacket

2. They didn't bring umbrellas. They didn't bring rain coats.

➡ They brought neither umbrellas nor coats

3. She doesn't buy a backpack. She doesn't buy a travel bag.

➡ She buys neither a backpack nor a travel bag

4. The guests don't ride cars. The guests don't ride motorbikes.

➡ The guests ride neither cars nor motorbikes.

5. Mr. Adi doesn't use the escalator. Mr. Adi doesn't use the lift.

➡ Mr. Adi uses neither the escalator nor the lift.

ELABORATION

Correlative Conjunction is a pair of phrases used to connect the sentence elements (phrase/clause) that have the equal information.

Neither-nor is used to negate two choices.

Pattern :

Neither + noun + nor+ noun

Exp.

Neither John nor Kathy has passed the English test.

Neither+adjective+nor+adjective

Exp.

She is neither shy nor naughty

Neither+verb+nor+verb

Exp.

He can neither write nor read.

Neither+clause+nor+clause

Exp.

Lucy neither brought a bag nor bought a sunglasses.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran Correlative Conjunction (konjungsi korelatif) dalam Bahasa Inggris yakni "Either ... or ..."

dan "Neither ... nor ..."

Kata Neither = Not Either = tidak keduanya

Kata Either = keduanya

Kata "Either ... or ..." digunakan dalam bentuk positif untuk menyatakan pilihan  dari kedua hal apakah ini atau itu:

(+) Subject + Modal Verb (Should/Will) + either + Verb-1 + or + Verb-1

Kata "Neither ... nor ..." digunakan dalam bentuk positif namun untuk menyatakan tidak dilakukannya pilihan dari kedua hal apakah ini atau itu:

(+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + neither + Verb-ing + nor + Verb-ing
