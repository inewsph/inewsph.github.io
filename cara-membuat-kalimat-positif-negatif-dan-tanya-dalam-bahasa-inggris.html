5. Buatlah kalimat negatif,positif dan kalimat tanya menggunakan kalimat air (Dalam bahasa inggris)


(+) = positive sentence/kalimat positif
(–) = negative sentence/kalimat negatif
(?) = interrogative sentence/kalimat tanya

(+) He is going to buy a bottle of water
(–) He is not going to buy a bottle of water
(?) Is he going to buy a bottle of water?
