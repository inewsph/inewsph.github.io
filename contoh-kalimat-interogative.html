Jawaban:

1.

(+) I sat on a bench. [Aku duduk di sebuah bangku]

(-) I didn't sit on a bench. [Aku tidak duduk di sebuah bangku]

(?) Did I sit on a bench? [Apakah aku duduk di sebuah bangku?]

2.

(+) We played basketball yesterday. [Kami bermain bola basket kemarin]

(-) We didn't play basketball yesterday. [Kami tidak bermain bola basket kemarin]

(?) Did we play basketball yesterday? [Apakah kita bermain bola basket kemarin?]

3.

(+) She sang so beautifully. [Dia bernyanyi dengan begitu indahnya]

(-) She didn't sing so beautifully. [Dia tidak bernyanyi dengan begitu indah]

(?) Did she sing so beautifully? [Apakah dia bernyanyi dengan begitu indahnya?]

4.

(+) They went to a library last week. [Mereka pergi ke sebuah perpustakaan minggu lalu]

(-) They didn't go to a library last week. [Mereka tidak pergi ke sebuah perpustakaan minggu lalu]

(?) Did they go to a library last week? [Apakah mereka pergi ke sebuah perpustakaan minggu lalu?]

5.

(+) She did her English homework last night. [Dia mengerjakan PR Bahasa Inggrisnya tadi malam]

(-) She didn't do her English homework last night. [Dia tidak mengerjakan PR Bahasa Inggrisnya tadi malam]

(?) Did she do her English homework last night? [Apakah dia mengerjakan PR Bahasa Inggrisnya tadi malam?]

Penjelasan:

Kalimat nomor 1-5 di jawaban dibuat menggunakan rumus kalimat verba Simple Past Tense. Rumusnya:

(+ / Positif) S + V2 + O

(- / Negatif) S + didn't + V1 + O

(? / Interogatif) Did + S + V1 + O?

SIMPLE PRESENT TENSE

Untuk subject i, you, they, we kalimat positifnya tidak menggunakan huruf (-s). Kalimat negatif nya menggunakan kata don't. Kemudian kalimat tanya nya menggunakan kata do.

Untuk subject he, she, it kalimat positifnya menggunakan huruf (-s). Kalimat negatif nya menggunakan kata doesn't. Kemudian kalimat tanya menggunakan kata does.

1. Simple Present Tense

Digunakan untuk menunjukkan fakta, kebiasaan, dan keadaan umum yang terjadi pada saat ini.

 

Fungsi Rumus Contoh

Positif Subject + Verb / Verb-s/es + Complement

Atau

Subject + am/are/is + Complement

a.       I work hard for this company.

b.      He works hard for this company.

c.       You are beautiful.

d.      She is beautiful.

Negatif Sunbect + do/does not + Verb1 + Complement

Atau

Subject + am/are/is + not + complement

a.       I do not work hard for this company.

b.      He does not work hard for this company.

c.       You are not beautiful.

d.      She is not beautiful.

Interogatif Do/does + Subject + Verb1 + Complement?

Atau

Am/are/is + Subject + Complement?

a.       Do I work hard for this company?

b.      Does he work hard for this company?

c.       Are you beautiful?

d.      Is she beautiful?

 

Kata sinyal waktu untuk tense ini adalah: usually, always, every, sometimes, normally, dan seldom, dll.

2. Present Continuous Tense

Digunakan untuk membicarakan aksi yang sedang berlangsung. Yang paling utama dari tense ini adalah adanya durasi waktu untuk kata kerjanya.

Fungsi Rumus Contoh

Positif Subject + am/are/is + Verb-ing + Complement

 

We are studying English.

Negatif Subject + am/are/is + Not + Complement We are not studying English.

Interogatif Am/are/is + Subject + Verb-ing + Complement? Are we studying English?

 

Kata sinyal waktu untuk tense ini adalah now, at the time, at this present, at this moment, dll.

3. Present Perfect Tense

Digunakan untuk menunjukkan suatu aksi atau kejadian sudah selesai dan menekankan pada hasilnya.

Fungsi Rumus Contoh

Positif Subject + has/have + Verb3 + Complement

 

a.       I have finished my homework.

b.      She has finished her homework.

Negatif Subject + has/have + not + Verb3 + Complement

 

a.       I have not finished my homework.

b.      She has not finished her homework.

Interogatif Has/have + Subject + Verb3 + Complement? a.       Have I finished my homework?

b.      Has she finished her homework?

 

Kata sinyal waktu untuk tense ini adalah already, ever, just, never, not yet, so far, till now, dll.

3. Present Perfect Continuous Tense

Digunakan untuk menunjukkan aksi yang dimulai pada masa lampau dan terus berlanjut sampai saat ini. Biasanya aksi yang menggunakan tense ini mempunyai durasi waktu tentu dan mempunyai relevansi dengan kondisi sekarang.

Fungsi Rumus Contoh

Positif Subject + has/have + been + Verb-ing + Complement

 

a.       You have been working here since 2015.

b.      She has been speaking for the last 3 hours.

Negatif Subject + has/have + not + been + Verb-ing + Complement

 

a.       You have not been working here since 2015.

b.      She has not been speaking for the last 3 hours.

Interogatif Has/have + Subject + been + Verb-ing + Complement? a.       Have you been working here since 2015?

b.      Has she been speaking for the last 3 hours?

Tenses dalam Bentuk “Past”

Kata sinyal untuk tense ini adalah since, for the past …, the whole week, dll.

6. Past Continuous Tense

Digunakan untuk menunjukkan suatu kejadian yang sedang terjadi pada masa lampau. Biasanya, ada 2 kejadian terjadi di masa lampau, dan kejadian yang diinterupsi tersebut yang menggunakan tense ini.

Fungsi Rumus Contoh

Positif Subject + was/were + Verb-ing + Complement

 

I was studying English when he called.

Negatif Subject + was/were + Not + Verb-ing + Complement I was not studying English when he called.

Interogatif Was/were + Subject + Verb-ing + Complement? Was I studying English when he called?

 

Kata sinyal waktu untuk tense ini adalah when, while, as long as, at this time yesterday, dll.


23. Apa yang dimaksud dengan "Posesive Adjective"? berikan pengertian masing- masing juga rumus dari kalimat positive,negative dan interogative juga contoh dari kalimat tersebut


Possessive adjective adalah determiner (special adjective) yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kepemilikan (possession) terhadap noun. Possessive adjective terdiri dari my, your, his, her, its, our, dan their.

Possessive adjective diletakkan di depan kata benda, misalnya: my book, your friend, our family, their house, his darling, her hair, its tail, etc.

Contoh:
- My book is new.
- I’m sure you don’t want to listen to all my problems.
- Your friend is studying now.
-Don’t worry. It’s not your fault.
- Our family is very friendly.
- You can stay at our house.
- Their house will be sold.
- They washed their faces and went to bed.
- His darling lives in Surabaya.
- Leo took off his coat and sat down.
- Her hair makes everybody interested.
- She looked at her watch.
- I have a cat; its tail is very long.
- Turn the box on its side.

Positive    : S + V1 / S + V1 + O
Negative    : S + do (es) not + V1
Interrogative    : Do (es) + S + V1 ?

- I drink milk.

28. Buatlah kalimat Positive, Negative, dan Interogative dari kalimat diatas​


Jawaban:

1. (+) Alex buys dinner on the way home.

(-) Alex does not buy dinner on the way home.

(?) Does Alex buy dinner on the way home?

2. (+) Her children help at home.

(-) Her children do not help at home.

(?) Do her children help at home?

Penjelasan:

Rumus Simple Present Tense:

Kalimat Positive (+)  :

Verbal     :  Subject + Verb1/Verb1+s/es + Object + adverb of time (every day / every morning)

Kalimat Negatif (-)    :

Verbal     :  Subject + do/does not +Verb1 + Object + adverb of time (every day / every morning)

Kalimat Interrogative (?)  :

Verbal    :  Do/Does + Subject + Verb1 + Object + adverb of time (every day / every morning) + ?

(-) S + do//does + not + verb 1 + Object

(?) Do//does + Subject + verb 1 + Object

He/She/It --> verb + -s/-es/-ies

I/You/They/We --> tidak ditambahkan apapun.
