6. buat 5 contoh giving advice menggunakan must​


Jawaban:

There are several sentence patterns used to express advice in English. The most common tense used to express advice is the usage of modal verb 'should'. Below are some examples of how to form sentences that express advice.

Discussion

Modals or modal verbs are words that come before a main verb and give it extra meaning such as advice, necessity, ability, possibility, and so on. We're going to focus on modals of advice. The modal pattern is modal + base verb.

There are three modals of advice in English: should, ought to, and had better.

Should

- Should is the most common modal of advice.

- We can use should for a positive sentence. Example: You should wake up early tomorrow.

- We can also use should for a negative sentence. Example: You shouldn't drink coffee five times a day.

- It's also okay for a question. Example: Should I go now?

Ought to

- It's more formal than should and is not that common to use.

- We can only use ought to in a positive sentence. Example: You ought to finish the project tonight.

Had better

- It is used to make stronger suggestion/advice.

- It can be used in a positive sentence. Example: You had better do your homework today.

- It can be used in a negative sentence too. Example: You had better not go to the party alone.
