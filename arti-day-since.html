10. Since she lived near the school, she made sure she was the first to be in class every day. Tentukan semua svonya dalam kalimat tersebut


Jawaban:

Since she lived near the school, she made sure she was the first to be in class every day.

Subject : since she lived near the school

verb : she made sure she was the first to be

object : In class every day

Penjelasan:

SVO merupakan singkatan dari subject, verb dan object.

- Subject merupakan pelaku yang melakukan sesuatu di dalam suatu kalimat.

- Verb dalam Bahasa Indonesia dikenal sebagai kata kerja.

- Object merupakan benda atau orang yang terkena dampak dari apa yang dilakukan si subject.
