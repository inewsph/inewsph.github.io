10. Dialog gabungan making complaint, suggestion, request, possibility dan giving instruction


A = Customer
B = Store Keeper

Situation: a customer is shopping in a boutique and wanted to try on some of her preferred cloth

A: "Excuse me, can i try this cloth ?" ➡ making request

B: "Sorry, madam. The cloth in these group can't be tried"

A: "But, there might be some flaws if i don't try it first" ➡ possibility

B: "No, it's impossible to find any flaws madam, since we only sold the best"

A: "This boutique is really horrible" ➡ complaint

B: "Sorry for the inconvenience, but it's the boss' policy

A: "You should tell your boss to let customer try on their cloth, you need to give us space" ➡ giving suggestion

B: "There are still plenty of cloth you can try on, just head to the left corner of this floor" ➡ giving instruction

15. dialog gabungan antara expression making complain,suggestion,request, dan giving instruction


Kelas: SMA
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: Suggestion, making complaint, giving instructions, request
Kata kunci: suggestion, complaint, request, instruction, dialogue
Pembahasan:

Nancy: Excuse me, Miss.I'd like to file a complaint about my class. (complaint)
Receptionist: I see, what seems to be the problem with your class?
N: I think it's too noisy, miss. There are too many boys in there. I can't concentrate well. So, do you think you could move me to the other class with the same level? (request)
R: Let me see, well, we do have one but it's rather late. It starts at 6.30 pm. Would that be fine for you?
N: Oh, OK, but I won't be able to come every Friday because I have another course. So, I can only come on Wednesday only. Can you help me catching up with the lesson?
R: Well, we certainly can ask the teacher to help you with that by tutoring session. Shall we book that session for you every saturday morning? (suggestion)
N: Yeah, that's a good idea.
R: OK, then, hold on. Let me get the document first upstairs. (to office boy) Fetch me the tutoring session document now, please! (instruction)
Office Boy: sure, Ma'am.
