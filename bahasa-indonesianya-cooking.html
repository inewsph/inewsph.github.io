27. Bahasa indonesia What was mother cooking


Jawaban:

What was mother cooking

-> Apa yang ibu telah masak

Penjelasan:

what = apa

was = adalah (V1 = is)

mother = ibu

cooking = memasak

Note:

The sentence in the question is using past continuous tense, which has the formula (S + was/were + V-ing).

Kalimat yang ada di pertanyaan nya itu merupakan "past continuous tense" dengan formula (S + Was/were + V-ing).
