NARRATIVE TEXT

Genre teks ini, pokok pembahasannya tentang cerita fiksi, mitos, legenda,  dan reality (berdasarkan kisah nyata seseorang).  ( Narrative text is a fiction and non-fiction composition which has the sequential plot and contains the moral value  )

KINDS OF NARRATIVE TEXTFabel (Fable)Mitos (Myth)Legend  ( Legenda )Folktales  ( Dongeng )SOCIAL FUNCTION/COMMUNICATIVE PURPOSE  

Narrative text disajikan dengan memiliki tujuan untuk menghibur para pembaca { to amuse / entertain the readers }

GENERIC STRUCTURE

{ Struktural dari Narrative text }

1. Orientation  

Paragraf ini terletak di awal pembukaan paragraf, mencakup latar belakang cerita, pengenalan tokoh, waktu terjadi perkara dan keterangan tempat yang ingin dipaparkan dalam cerita.   ( Orientation is the very beginning part of paragraph which contains the background of the story begins ).

2. Complication

Paragraf selanjutnya memuat  konflik permasalahan -permasalahan yang timbul pada tokoh-tokoh utamanya. ( Complication contains the problem is arisen toward the main characters in the story )

3. Resolution

Paragraf ini memuat  penyelesaian akhir dari permasalahan yang muncul, apakah berakhir happy ending atau sad ending. ( Resolution contains how the main characters try to solve their problem. The last result determines  to the good ending or bad ending or even suspend  )

4. Coda

Berisi tentang pesan-pesan moral positive yang dapat disimpulkan dari kisah/cerita yang disajikan. Bagian Coda ini, tidak selalu sering dipaparkan dalam semua narrative text. ( Coda contains the message or moral value that can be taken from the story. It is usually put on the last paragraph )

LANGUAGE FEATURES

Penggunaan keterangan waktu yang spesifik "Once upon a time .........". Pada umumnya dicantumkan di awal pembukaan paragraf utama. ( It uses the adverb of time )Penggunaan struktur kalimat simple past tense ( kata kerja bentukan lampau ). ( It uses simple past tense )Memuat percakapan - percakapan dengan menggunakan kalimat langsung ( direct speech ) bentuk present tense. ( It contains the direct speech )

Example :

Dewi Mutiara said , "Your daughter will bring a bad luck to whole country ."

Penggunaan konjungsi waktu ( when, suddenly, then.... ). ( It uses the time conjunction )Penggunaan nama-nama tokoh  hewan dan ataupun nama -nama tokoh  fiktif yang bersifat tidak umum . ( It uses the specific character )Penggunaan kata kerja aksi. ( it uses the action verbs )

Narrative adalah cerita khayal yang bertujuan menghibur pembaca. Tentu yang namanya cerita khayal itu ya belum tentu keberannya karena bisa jadi itu hanyalah imaginasi atau cerita fiktif yang dibuat oleh seseorang atau cerita buatan sekelompok masyarakat yang tida terbukti kebenarannya. Contoh narrative yaitu, Cinderella, Sangkurian, Snow White, Rabbit and Crocodile, dsb. Untuk penjelasan lebih rinci silahkan baca penjelasan berikut.

Definition of Narrative Text

A narrative text is an imaginative story to entertain people (teks narasi adalah cerita imaginatif yang bertujuan menghibur orang).

Generic Structure of Narrative Text

# Orientation : It is about the opening paragraph where the characters of the story are introduced.(berisi pengenalan tokoh, tempat dan waktu terjadinya cerita (siapa atau apa, kapan dan dimana)

# Complication : Where the problems in the story developed. (Permasalahan muncul / mulai terjadi dan berkembang)

# Resolution : Where the problems in the story is solved. Masalah selesai, secara baik “happy ending” ataupun buruk “bad ending”.

# Coda / reorientation (optional) – lesson from the story.

Kadangkala susunan (generic structure) narrative text bisa berisi: Orientation, Complication, Evaluation, Resolution dan Reorientation. Meski “Evaluation” dan “Reorientation” merupakan optional; bisa ditambahkan dan bisa tidak. Evaluation berisi penilaian/evaluasi terhadap jalannya cerita atau konflik. Sedangkan Reorientation berisi penyimpulan isi akhir cerita.

Purpose of Narrative Text

– The Purpose of Narrative Text is to amuse or to entertain the reader with a story.

The Characteristics / Language Feature of Narrative Text

– Past tense (killed, drunk, etc)

– Adverb of time (Once upun a time, one day, etc)

– Time conjunction (when, then, suddenly, etc)

– Specific character. The character of the story is specific, not general. (Cinderella, Snow White, Alibaba, etc)

– Action verbs. A verb that shows an action. (killed, dug, walked, etc)

– Direct speech. It is to make the story lively. (Snow White said,”My name is Snow White). The direct speech uses present tense.

Contoh of Narrative Text

Contoh Narrative Text tentang Fable beserta terjemahannya

The Rabbit and the Turtle

One day a rabbit was boasting about how fast he could run. He was laughing at the turtle for being so slow.

Much to the rabbit’s surprise, the turtle challenged him to a race. The rabbit thought this was a good joke and accepted the challenge. The fox was to be the umpire of the race. As the race began, the rabbit raced way ahead of the turtle, just like everyone thought.

The rabbit got to the halfway point and could not see the turtle anywhere. He was hot and tired and decided to stop and take a short nap. All this time the turtle kept walking step by step by step. He never quit no matter how hot or tired he got. He just kept going.

However, the rabbit slept longer than he had thought and woke up. He could not see the turtle anywhere! He went at full-speed to the finish line but found the turtle there waiting for him.

29. What is the Narrative text? What is function of Narrative text? What are the generic structures of Narrative text? What is the language function of Narrative text ? Explain briefly abaut (artikan dalam B Indonesia) fairytales Short stories Fables Folk tales Legends Myths


Definition of Narrative Text

Narrative text is an imaginative story to entertain people (teks narasi adalah cerita imaginatif yang bertujuan untuk menghibur orang).

Function

To entertain people (untuk menghibur orang).

Generic Structure of Narrative Text

➪ Orientation : It is about the opening paragraph where the characters of the story are introduced.(berisi pengenalan tokoh, tempat dan waktu terjadinya cerita (siapa atau apa, kapan dan dimana)

➪ Complication : Where the problems in the story developed. (Permasalahan muncul / mulai terjadi dan berkembang)

➪ Resolution : Where the problems in the story is solved. Masalah selesai, secara baik “happy ending” ataupun buruk “bad ending”.

➪ Coda / reorientation (optional) – lesson from the story.

The Characteristics / Language Feature of Narrative Text

– Past tense (killed, drunk, etc)

– Adverb of time (Once upun a time, one day, etc)

– Time conjunction (when, then, suddenly, etc)

– Specific character. The character of the story is specific, not general. (Cinderella, Snow White, Alibaba, etc)

– Action verbs. A verb that shows an action. (killed, dug, walked, etc)

– Direct speech. It is to make the story lively. (Snow White said,”My name is Snow White). The direct speech uses present tense.
