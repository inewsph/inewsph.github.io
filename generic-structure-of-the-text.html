8. Explain the generic structure of descriptive text


First, Descriptive Text is . . . .
~> Descriptive text is a text that describes a particular person, place or thing.

Examples describe about:
· Landscape description.
· Observation.
· Speech.
· etc.

Second, the purposes/functions of “Descriptive Text”

~> To describes a characteristic for person, place or thing in detial.

Third, the structure of the text/generic structure.
1. Identification.
~> In this part introduces to the subject of the description.
2. Description.
~> In this part gives details of the characteristic features of the subject. It may describe parts,
qualities, characteristies, size, physical apperance, ability, habit, daily live, etc.
3. Conclution (optional).

Fourth, Language Feature.
~> Descriptive text use:
· Simple present tense ~> If things/persons descried are still alive.
· Simple past tense ~> If things/persons descried do not exist anymore.

· Use noun.
· Use kinds of adjective.
· Use active verb.
· Use allusion, imagination language.

Fifth, the example of Descriptive text.

My grandmother.
Identification ~> introduces the person described.

My grandmother is a very gentle, loving, and caring person. She never raises her voice at anyone. She has lived with me for as long as I can remember. She cakes care of me when mom and go to work.

Description ~> gives the details of the person described.

My grandmother is a very neat and tidy person. She has very dry grey hair which she usually pulls up into a bun. She has dark brown eyes that twinkle whenever she sees me. I hardly ever see them wet.

Grandmother likes to tell stories. She usually tells me brief stories of her childhood and expresses them very well with her tired, old, wrinkled hands. Sometimes, she also tells story about grandfather who has pass away. My dear grandmother always says good things about him. She once told me that he was the nicest person she had ever met.


Clonclution ~> optional statement to sum up main ideas.

I really love my grandmother.

24. Find out the generic structure of the text!


generic structure of the text:

1. orientation: introducing the participants and informing the time and the place

2. complication: describing the rising crises which the participants have to do with

3. resolution: showing the way of participant to solve the crises, better or worse


example

narrative text dari snow white

(Orientation)
Once upon a time there lived a little girl named Snow White. She lived with her Aunt and Uncle because her parents were dead.

(Complication)
One day she heard her Uncle and Aunt talking about leaving Snow White in the castle because they both wanted to go to America and they didn’t have enough money to take Snow White.

Snow White did not want her Uncle and Aunt to do this so she decided it would be best if she ran away. The next morning she ran away from home when her Aunt and Uncle were having breakfast. She ran away into the woods.

(Resolution)
Then she saw this little cottage. She knocked but no one answered so she went inside and fell asleep.
Meanwhile, the seven dwarfs were coming home from work. They went inside. There they found Snow White sleeping. Then Snow White woke up. She saw the dwarfs. The dwarfs said, “what is your name?” Snow White said, “My name is Snow White.”

Doc, one of the dwarfs, said, “If you wish, you may live here with us.” Snow White said, “Oh could I? Thank you.” Then Snow White told the dwarfs the whole story and finally Snow White and the 7 dwarfs lived happily ever after.

29. what is the generic structure of the text ?


“What is the generic structure of the text?” adalah suatu pertanyaan yang digunakan untuk menanyakan struktur teks bacaan yang diberikan. Soal ini tidak memberikan teks bacaan yang dimaksud sehingga saya tidak dapat memberi jawaban tepatnya. Berikut beberapa jenis teks beserta generic structurenya.

Penjelasan:

Teks merupakan suatu wacana yang diwujudkan dalam bentuk tulisan. Dalam Bahasa Inggris terdapat 5 jenis teks yang umum digunakan, yaitu descriptive text, narrative text, report text, explanation text, dan recount text.

Jenis-jenis Text

1. Descriptive Text

Descriptive text ialah jenis teks yang digunakan untuk menggambarkan seseorang atau suatu objek secara umum. Generic structurenya ialah sebagai berikut:

Identification, pembuka teks yang berisi pengenalan mengenai objek yang akan dijelaskan lebih lanjut. Description, berisi gambaran objek misalnya ukuran, warna, dan karakteristik khusus.

2. Narrative Text

Narrative text adalah jenis teks yang digunakan untuk menceritakan suatu kisah (umumnya fiksi) yang disusun secara kronologis. Generic structure narrative text ialah sebagai berikut:

Orientation, berisi pengenalan tokoh dan latar cerita. Complication, berisi permasalahan yang dihadapi oleh para tokoh dalam cerita. Resolution, berisi pemecahan masalah yang dihadapi pada bagian complication. Re-orientation, bagian penutup cerita yang bersifat opsional dan berisi pesan dari penulis mengenai cerita.

3. Report Text

Report text adalah teks yang membahas suatu objek lebih detail dibandingkan descriptive text. Generic structure:

General classification, berisi gambaran umum mengenai objek yang akan dibahas. Description, berisi penjelasan lebih lanjut dari objek yang dikenalkan.

4. Explanation text

Explanation text merupakan teks yang menjelaskan proses terjadinya suatu fenomena. Beriku generic structurenya:

General statement, berisi gambaran umum mengenai objek atau fenomena yang akan dijelaskan. Explanation, berisi serangkaian peristiwa mengenai proses terjadinya objek atau fenomena yang dikenalkan. Closing, penutup teks yang bersifat opsional dan berisi informasi tambahan mengenai topik yang dibahas.

5. Recount Text

Recount text adalah jenis teks yang menceritakan pengalaman seseorang di masa lampau. Berikut generic structurenya:

Orientation, berisi pengenalan tokoh serta latar cerita. Event, berisi serangkaian peristiwa yang terjadi. Re-orientation, penutup cerita yang bersifat opsional dan berisi kesimpulan keseluruhan cerita.
