5. Buat dialog untuk memberi saran dan saran tentang masalah berikut. Saya ingin menjadi pengguna youtube tetapi saya hanya memiliki smartphone. Contoh dialog?.. Tolong bantu ya kak​


Giving Advice and Suggestion

Hans: You look like as if you're thinking of something. What happened?

Leo: Well, nothing. Actually I was wondering about starting my Youtube channel.

Hans: You mean becoming a Youtuber?

Leo: Kind of. But I doubt it because I don't have fancy equipment like cameras, microphone and editing softwares.

Hans: But you do have your smartphone, right?

Leo: Only it.

Hans: Look, there are many people out there who starts their career on Youtube using their smartphone only. If they can do it, then why can't you? But in order to reach the success level, you must be diligent about uploading video and improving your video by the time. I suggest start doing it soon!

Leo: Hmm, sounds encouraging. I will try my best.

Hans: And you should let me know when you'll publish your first video. I'll send you a like!

Leo: Thank you, bro.

Pembahasan

Banyak cara yang dapat digunakan untuk menyatakan kritik dan saran dalam bahasa inggris. Beberapa diantaranya adalah:

RECOMMEND / SUGGEST + ING

Kamu dapat menggunakan format verb+ing setelah kata recommend/suggest. Contohnya “It’s raining outside, I recommend taking an umbrella” or “I suggest taking an umbrella.”

RECOMMEND / SUGGEST + (THAT) + SUBJECT + VERB

Atau kamu juga bisa menggunakan struktur ini: “I recommend that you take an umbrella” atau “I suggest that you take an umbrella.”

OTHER ENGLISH PHRASES FOR SUGGESTIONS

Kalian juga bisa memberikan saran menggunakan kata selain recommend atau suggest. Contoh sederhananya adalah menggunakan “you should” atau “you shouldn’t”. Contoh dalam kalimat: “You should/You shouldn’t.” Akan tetapi menggunakan kata should terdengar sedikit memberi kesan memerintah. Oleh karena itu jika kamu berbicara dengan seseorang yang tidak kamu kenal secara dekat, ada cara lain yang bisa digunakan yaitu “You might want to” or “You might not want to.”

Jawaban:

dialog tentang kewajiban peraturan sekolah

udin: Hey, Good morning, Yunita!

Yunita: Hello, udin! Good morning. How are you?

udin: Wow, I am very well thank you. How about you? I don’t see you very often to be in school so early in the morning.

Yunita: I am fine too, udin. I’m just afraid I would come late if I didn’t come so early.

udin: But, I think you never come this early before, Yunita. It’s just 06.00 AM now.

Yunita: I have moved to Teluk Betung and it is so far from our school.

udin: Ooo… That’s a smart decision, Yunita. You know about our school regulation, right?

Yunita: Yeah! We will be locked outside the school gate if we come late with any reason. Do you usually arrive at school this early?

udin: Yes, Yunita. I come this early to school everyday because I’m one of the students’ union. So, I was assigned to come earlier to school everyday and give a good attitude for every students in our school.

Yunita: Wow, good! By the way, what do you think of our school regulation? Is that good enough for every student?

udin: Humm… I think so. What’s up Yunita? Do you have any problem?

Yunita: Yeah, you’re right, udin. Now, my home is so far from our school. So, I need to bring my phone to call my father. I will be picked up by him. You know what I mean?

udin: I know. But, I have no idea. May be you can talk to Mr. Gunawan. He is the head of students’ union? Maybe, you could get exception from him.

Yunita: I will try. But, I think my father will be better to talk about this.  

udin: That’s good! I have to go now. See you Yunita! Bye!

 

Yunita: Bye!
