6. Sebutkan 5 contoh kalimat possessive adjective dan possessive pronouns


Untuk membuat kalimat possessive adjective dan possessive pronouns kita harus memperhatikan kalimat dengan menggunakan subjective pronoun terlebih dahulu. Contoh kalimat possessive adjective dan possessive pronouns adalah

1. I have a new bag.

Possessive adjective: My bag is blue.

Possessive pronoun: This blue bag is mine.

2. You have two cat.

Possessive adjective: Your cats are very cute.

Possessive pronoun: Those cute cats are yours.

3. He always waters the plants everyday.

Possessive adjective: His plants are very fertile.

Possessive pronoun: These plants are his.

4. We play kites together.

Possessive adjective: Our kites fly so high.

Possessive pronoun: Ours have many colours and beautiful.

5. They have funny rabbits.

Possessive adjective: Their rabbits play in the backyard.

Possessive pronoun:  Theirs eat a lot of carrots.

Pembahasan

Penjelasan possessive adjective dan possessive pronoun dalam bahasa Inggris, yaitu

Possessive adjectives are words that indicate possession. Possessive adjectives are used with nouns. (Possessive adjectives adalah kata-kata yang mengindikasikan kepemilikan. Possessive adjectives digunakan bersamaan dengan kata benda atau nomina).Possessive pronouns also show possession but possessive pronouns are not followed by nouns. (Possessive pronouns juga menunjukkan kepemilikan tetapi tidak diikuti dengan kata benda atau nomina).Pelajari lebih lanjutMateri tentang soal tentang object pronoun pada https://brainly.co.id/tugas/8883784Materi tentang contoh teks yang mengandung pronoun pada https://brainly.co.id/tugas/25913730Materi tentang the meaning of possessive pronoun pada http://brainly.co.id/tugas/7834207Detail Jawaban

- My dad never speaks loudly. (Ayah saya tidak pernah berbicara dengan keras.)

2.

A transitive verb is a verb that can take a direct object. In other words, the action of a transitive verb is done to someone or something. Most verbs are transitive. A transitive verb contrasts with an intransitive verb, which is a verb that does not take a direct object.

terjemahan :

Kata kerja transitif adalah kata kerja yang dapat mengambil objek langsung. Dengan kata lain, tindakan kata kerja transitif dilakukan pada seseorang atau sesuatu. Kebanyakan kata kerja bersifat transitif. Kata kerja transitif kontras dengan kata kerja intransitif, yaitu kata kerja yang tidak mengambil objek langsung.

3.

Possesive adjectives is a word that indicates the possession of the noun to a person/a few people. The possessive adjectives are my, our, your, his, their, her, and its.

Examples of Possessive Adjective:

- My computer is not working as fast as it worked in the beginning.

- Our father told us not to quarrel with anyone.

terjemahan :

Kata sifat possesif adalah kata yang menunjukkan kepemilikan kata benda kepada seseorang / beberapa orang. Kata sifat posesif adalah my, our, your, his, their, her, and its.


14. Membuat kalimat tentang possessive adjective


 possesive adjective adalah kalimat milik/kepunyaan misal
-Yours is bigger than his
-This is my book.
Possessive Adjective digunakan dan diikuti oleh kata benda-nya (nouns), contohnya: my book, your car, their class, our teacher, his pen, her umbrella, its tail, etc.
Contoh :

These clothes are very nice. Mine is the expensive one.
Artinya: Baju-baju ini sangat bagus. (Baju) Milik saya yang harganya mahal.

This is your pen, and the red one is mine.
Artinya: Ini pulpen mu, dan yang warna merah adalah (pulpen) milik saya.
