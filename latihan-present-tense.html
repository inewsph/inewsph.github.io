PEMBAHASAN

✎ Simple Present Tense

☛ Digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu pekerjaan yang dilakukan secara berulang-ulang atau sudah menjadi suatu kebiasaan

☛ Pola Simple Present Tense

(+) subject + verb1 + object + complement(-) subject + do/does + not + verb1 + object + complement(?) do/does + subject + verb1 + object + complement

☛ Catatan

Do digunakan untuk subjek I, you, we, they, dan nama orang banyak (misal: Rina dan Rini) Does digunakan untuk subjek he, she, it dan nama orang tunggal (misal: Rini)Pada kalimat positif, jika subjeknya he, she, it dan nama orang tunggal (misal: Rini), maka kata kerja harus ditambah "s/es"

✎ Simple Present Continuous Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu pekerjaan yang sedang di lakukan pada masa sekarang.

Rumus:

(+) S + to be + V-ing + O + C

(-) S + to be + not + V-ing + O + C

(?) To be+ S + V-ing + O + C + ?

Pengelompokan Subject Berdasarkan To be

am : Iis : He, She, It, dan Nama Orang Tunggal (Misal: Rini) are : You, We, They, dan Nama Orang Banyak (Misal: Rini dan Rina)

✎ Simple Past Tense

☛ Digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu pekerjaan yang dilakukan di waktu lampau. Misalnya,

menceritakan pengalamankejadian kemarinkejadian beberapa hari yang lalukejadian beberapa minggu yang lalu, dan seterusnya

☛ Pola Simple Present Tense

(+) subject + verb2 + object + complement(-) subject + did + not + verb1 + object + complement(?) did + subject + verb1 + object + complement

☛ Catatan

Did digunakan untuk semua subjek

Present continous tense

Rumus:

Subject + be (is/am/are) + verb-ing

Contoh:

She is dancing in the rain

They are eating at Scott’s favorite restaurant today

I'm reading this book right now

We are trying to bake the cake

Rumus:

Subject + was/were + present Participle (V-ing)

Contoh:

He was sleeping

The people were waiting

Emma was watching television

( simple present tense ) Passive voice : _____________________________________ 2. Active voice : Dika writes poem. ( simple present tense ) Passive voice : ______________________________________ 3. Active voice : The students clean the classroom. ( simple present tense ) Passive voice : ______________________________________ 4. Active voice : My father bought a car yesterday. ( past tense ) Passive voice : ______________________________________ 5. Active voice : Karyo washed a motor this morning. ( past tense ) Passive voice :

30. tugas hari ini adalah latihan.1. Saya dan andi makan es krim2. Mereka berenang di kolam.3. Ayah bekerja di sawah4. Ibu membeli sayur ke pasar.Buat kalimat di atas menjadi kalimat simple present tense, present continuous tense dan past continuous tense masing-masing dalam bentuk positif, negatif dan introgatif.


Jawaban:

1. (+) I eat ice cream with Andi

(-) = I did not eat ice cream with Andi

(?) = Did I eat ice cream with Andi ?

present continuous tense : (+) I am eating ice cream with Andi

(-) I am not eating ice cream with Andi

(?) am I eating ice cream with Andi ?

past continuous tense :

(+) = I was eating ice cream with Andi

(-) = I wasn't eating ice cream with Andi

(?) Wasn't I eating ice cream with Andi ?

2. (+) = They swim in the pool

(-) = They did not swim in the pool

(?) = Did they swim in the pool ?

present continuous tense.

(+) = They are swimming in the pool

(-) = They are not swimming in the pool

(?) = Are they swimming in the pool ?

past continuous tense.

(+) They were swimming in the pool

(-) = They weren't swimming in the pool

(?) = Weren't they swimming in the pool ?

3. (+) = Dad works at the fields

(-) = dad does not work at the fields

(?) = Did dad work at the fields ?

present continuous tense.

(+) dad is working at the fields

(-) dad is not working at the fields

(?) is dad working at the fields ?

past continuous tense.

(+) dad was working at the fields

(-) dad was not working at the fields

(?) wasn't dad working at the fields ?

4. (+) Mom buys vegetables at the market

(-) mom doesn't buy vegetables at the market

(?) did mom buy vegetables at the market ?

present continuous tense.

(+) Mom is buying vegetables at the market

(-) Mom isn't buying vegetables at the market

(?) Isn't mom buying vegetables at the market ?

Past continuous tense.

(+) Mom was buying vegetables at the market

(-) mom wasn't buying vegetables at the market

(?) wasn't mom buying vegetables at the market ?
