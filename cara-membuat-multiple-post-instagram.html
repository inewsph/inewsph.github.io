21. 1. How to download music from youtube? 2. How to post picture on instagram? pliss lagi butuh banget​


Jawaban:

1.Type the title of your favorite song in the search field using the YouTube application.

Click on the video.

Click 'Download' with the down arrow icon.

Select audio only to save the sound file or video quality. Select 'ok'

2. 1# The first step we have to do is login to our Instagram account.

2# After logging in, make a post in the feed by uploading a photo.

3# When selecting photos to upload there is a Select Multiple option, click to create multiple posts.

4# The next step is to select the photos we want to upload. Currently, Instagram limits the number of photos uploaded to a maximum of 10 photos.

6# When you have finished selecting photos, tap Next.

7# When finished setting filters and editing, tap Next to proceed to the next step.

8# In the next step, we add a caption, where the photo was taken, and tag the people we want. Giving these descriptions once is enough for all photos.

9# Next, tap Share to share the photos.

10# Done, a number of photos have been published into one post on the Instagram feed. Good luck.
