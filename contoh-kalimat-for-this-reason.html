6. Tulislah arti kata" berikut dan buatkan kalimat dengen benar.a. thus b. for this reasonc. consequentlyd. as a result​


Jawaban:

a) "thus" means as a result of something / consequences.

e.g. -> I failed the class and thus I have to join summer school.

b) "for this reason" means a reason / explaination for something that happens(?) idk bruv

e.g. -> It is partly for this reason that he's refusing to rush any recruitment moves.

c) "consequently" means as a result. xD

e.g. -> She was an intelligent student and consequently did well at school.

d) "as a result" means the cause and effect of something.

e.g. -> Several houses are damaged as a result of the earthquake.

Jawaban:

Throw away culture has been established for quite some time in Indonesia, and its not going away anytime soon.

The amount of freedom that is given to the general population and the increasing culture of consumerism promote the throw away culture. The lack of discipline, established rule, and general conscience in the community can also enhance the throw away culture. For example, cleanliness is a public responsibility in Singapore, people doesnt just throw trash around the street becasue they know : 1. its wrong, 2. its bad 3. its an established culture among the community. Whereas in Indonesia, alot of the times people just doesnt care.

People can freely buy and consume disposable single use products such as food packaging and general household items that require little worry when it comes to disposing the product, therefore people can just throw away and discard the product easily and carelessly.

people can start to fight against this culture by increasing the awareness toward the society, giving out steps on how to combat this, and giving an example by doing it, so that your actions may be noticed by people and be followed. We can also stop on using single use products and change to using more reusable product.
