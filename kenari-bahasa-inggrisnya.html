20. Deskripsi tentang burung kenari dalam bhs inggris beserta artinya


Canary (Serinus Canaria) was first discovered by French sailor Jean de Berthan Cout in the Canary Islands in the 15th century. Impressed because of the fur and sonority of his voice. Diversity canary is now the development of the descendants of wild canary named latin Serinus Canarius. The number of this type is the natural condition or because of interbreeding happened since five centuries ago. Dutch national who is now better known as the country of export walnut Indonesia, originally brought walnuts from the UK, Germany and Belgium then walnuts are mated with wild canary and produce several types such as norwich, scoth fancy, bolder fancy, london fancy, yorkshire, lanchaisire, gloser, etc. Name canary is not related to walnuts, but after the island on their natural habitat, the Canary Islands. Islands itself got its name from the Latin word meaning canarias (island) any dog, because the number of stray dogs that were there in ancient times.

Canary (Serinus Canaria) pertama kali ditemukan oleh pelaut Prancis Jean de Berthan Cout di Kepulauan Canary pada abad ke-15. Terkesan karena bulu dan nyaring suaranya. Keanekaragaman burung kenari kini berkembang dari keturunan burung kenari liar bernama latin Serinus Canarius. Jumlah jenis ini adalah kondisi alamiah atau karena perkawinan silang terjadi sejak lima abad yang lalu. Warga negara Belanda yang kini lebih dikenal dengan negara kenari ekspor Indonesia, asalnya membawa kenari dari Inggris, Jerman dan Belgia kemudian kenari dikawinkan dengan burung kenari liar dan menghasilkan beberapa jenis seperti norwich, sarang fancy, fancy yang lebih mewah, london fancy, yorkshire. , Lanchaisire, gloser, dll. Nama kenari tidak berhubungan dengan kenari, tapi setelah pulau di habitat aslinya, Kepulauan Canary. Pulau itu sendiri mendapat namanya dari kata Latin yang berarti canarias (pulau) anjing mana pun, karena banyaknya anjing liar yang ada di zaman purba.

27. deskripsikan burung kenari dgn bahasa Inggris


 I have a lovely canary bird. His name is Kanari. He is fluffy little bird. His head and breast are covered by bright yellow plumage, and his wings and tail feathers are greenish-yellow. He looks beautiful with all those colors.

I put Kanari into a medium bird cage to give him some free space. I usually feed him two times a day. Sometimes I feed him bird food, larvae, crickets, or grains. He likes larvae and cricket most. I also provide him a little freshwater in a small container inside his cage to let her drink. Every morning I place his cage outside in front of my house to keep him warm. He is a great songbird. I love listening to his chirping, it’s so beautiful and pleasant.
