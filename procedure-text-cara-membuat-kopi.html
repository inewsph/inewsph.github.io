Chocolate coffee

Ingredients:

1.    1 tablespoon instant coffee
2.    1 tablespoon chocolate powder
3.    450 ml hot milk
4.    2 tablespoons sugar
5.    50 grams ice cubes

How to make:

1.    Prepare a glass or a cup. Put sugar, instant coffee, and chocolate powder into the glass/cup. Stir it.
2.    Add hot milk, and stir it again until mixed well.
3.    After it is cold, add ice cubes.
4.    Finally, chocolate coffee is ready to serve.

Make a procedure text about sunny side up egg!

(Buatlah teks prosedur tentang membuat telur mata sapi!)

English Version                                         Sunny Side Up

Ingredients

- Egg

- Butter / Oil

- Spatula

- Pan

- Plate

Steps

1. Put the pan on the stove and put some butter/oil

2. Wait until the butter melted or the oil is hot

3. Crack the egg on top of the stove

4. wait until all egg whites and the yolk is done

5. Take the spatula and scrap the bottom side of the egg

6. Serve on a plate while hot

Indonesian Version (For Translate Purposes Only)                           Cara Membuat Telur Mata Sapi

Bahan

- Telur

- Mentega/Minyak

- Spatula

- Panci

- Piring

Langkah - Langkah

1. Simpan panci di kompor lalu masukkan mentega/minyak

2. Tunggu hingga mentega mencair atau minyak panas

3. Pecahkan telur di panci

4. Tunggu hingga seluruh bagian putih dan kuning telur matang

5. Ambil spatula lalu masukkan spatula dibawah telur

6. Sajikan di piring selagi hangat

PEMBAHASAN

Procedure Text is a text that gives people how to use/make something. It is a social function how to describe making something completely with sequence of steps.

(Teks Prosedur adalah teks yang memberikan orang informasi tentang cara menggunakan/membuat sesuatu. Ini memiliki fungsi sosial yakni bagaimana cara menjelaskan sesuatu menggunakan sepenuhnya langkah-langkah.)

Procedure Text Structure

(Struktur Teks Prosedur)

a. Goal (Title) [How to make..., How to use..., etc.]

(Tujuan (Judul) [Cara membuat..., Cara memakai..., dll.])

b. Ingredients (Bahan)

c. Steps (Langkah-Langkah)

Purpose to make a Procedure Text

(Tujuan membuat Teks Prosedur)

a. To inform the readers how to make something or how to operate something.

(Untuk memberitahu pembaca cara membuat atau menggunakan sesuatu.)

How to make a black forest cake
Ingredients :
1. 150 grams of sugar
2. 100 grams of wheat flour
3. 100 grams of butter
4. 200 grams whipped cream powder
5. 60 grams of cocoa powder
6. 40 grams of cornstarch
7. 10 red strawberries
8. 8 chicken eggs
9. 1 can of black cherry
10. ¼ teaspoon vanilla

steps :
1. Combine the sugar and eggs, shake until changes to be lumpy. Combine chocolate and flour, then stir until well blended.
2. Give a little melted butter while you are stirring the ingredients.
3. Grease by using margarine and sprinkle the flour on it. After that, pour into the baking dish which is about 20 cm. Put it in the oven with 180 Celsius as the temperature and do it for approximately 50 minutes.
4. Cut the cake up into two sections apart. Grease and give the cherry on the two hemispheres.
5. Brush the entire body of the cake with whipped cream and decorate with cherry fruit. Put in the fridge for 10-15 minutes
6. After that, Black Forest cake is ready to eat.

How to Make the Delicious and Savory “Selat Solo”

Ingredients for the stew (soup):

1 whole clove

1 whole nutmeg

100 ml of soy sauce

1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce

Salt to taste

1 clove thinly sliced garlic

½ teaspoon ground pepper

1 tablespoon margarine

30 gr thinly sliced onion

Materials for the content and complementary:

100 g boiled carrot (cut lengthwise)

100 g pickled cucumber

4 pieces of lettuce

100 grams of fries

2 boiled eggs which have been peeled

200 grams of meat

100 grams of boiled beans cut lengthwise

Ingredients for the sauce mustard:

1 teaspoon sugar

Salt to taste

1 teaspoon of cooking vinegar

1 boiled yolks which has been smoothed

1 teaspoon mustard paste

How to make selat solo:

Meat that has been cleaned up, is cut into thin crosswise ¾ cm.

Heat the margarine until melted and saute garlic and onion until fragrant.

Insert the meat and stir until the meat changes its color.

Next, enter the soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce; do not forget to add salt and cloves, nutmeg and pepper.

Add the water and cook until boiling.

Enter the boiled eggs

After the meat is cooked and gravy shrinks, lift it up and set it aside.

To make the mustard sauce, all the sauce ingredients are mixed and stir until it is evenly distributed.

For the Serving:

Arrange the lettuce, boiled vegetables, and fries.

Add the sliced meat and give pickles.

Add the eggs and mustard sauce.

Flush with a splash of gravy and selat solo is ready to serve.

20. Text procedure cara membuat benda


How To Make A Kite

Materials Needed :
A2 paper
String (thick)
Pipe cleaners / sticks
Scissors
Colored pencils (optional)
Tape (optional)

Steps :
1. Get a piece of paper (any color), and cut a diamond shape from it. Sometimes it is better if you tape four pieces together. But you have to tape them together the right way. Tape two together, then tape together the other two. Tape them on each other.
2. To cut a diamond shape on the paper (if you are not taping four sheets together), cut the 4 corners off. It should be in the shape of a square. the paper horizontally.
3. Repeat step 2 but this time lay it across the paper vertically.
4. Tie the two sticks or pipe cleaners together. Make it as tight as possible. 5.Pierce 2 holes in the diamond-shaped paper. On the inside corner where the two sticks cross, thread a thick string.
6. Using the other end of the thick string, do the same. Then tie the string when both of the ends of the string are threaded through.
7. Tie a long and strong string onto the sticks or pipe cleaners to form the tail. You can add small strips of paper to the end of this tail string for added buoyancy and weighting. It also looks more complete.
8. Color the kite if you want.
9. Find a breeze or some light wind, have someone to help you hold your kite as you wait for wind to come.
10. Have fun flying your kite.
